# Chapter 29: Seeking Aid

`Li Xiangping was instantly startled. Seeing Wan Yuankai's face full of smiles, his thoughts spun as he said to himself:`

`*The Wan family is being a bit too enthusiastic. I need to keep my guard up.*`

`His outward smile remained unchanged` as he cupped his hands toward Wan Yuankai, shaking his head repeatedly. "Your esteemed father is too polite! I absolutely cannot accept this!"

Wan Yuankai shook his head and retrieved a small pine wood box from his sleeve. Opening the polished lid, it revealed a pale green fruit resting `on a cloth lining`.

The fruit's surface was covered in a smooth, keratinous layer akin to snake scales. The interplay of pale green and white reflected a faint luminescence under the sunlight, giving it a truly remarkable appearance.

Wan Yuankai, however, wore a look of embarrassment as he whispered, "My Wan family is beset by difficulties both internal and external, spending more than we earn year after year. This Songyue fruit offers some minor benefits for shaping the Bearing Brightness Wheel, but such a trifle is hardly worth mentioning. I implore Brother Li to accept it, so I might return and report my mission accomplished."

Seeing Li Xiangping examining the fruit, Wan Yuankai suddenly realized something and hurriedly explained, "You simply slice the skin with a small knife and drink the juice within."

Li Xiangping stared at the fruit, his mind constantly weighing the situation.

He thought to himself, _If the Wan family is truly beset by difficulties inside and out, why tell me? Isn't that just inviting unwarranted greed?_

_Forget it! Regardless of what game he's playing, I should observe the situation first!_

"Then it would be impolite for me to refuse!"

Li Xiangping laughed heartily. He watched as Li Yesheng stepped forward to receive the box and carefully carry it away before shifting the conversation.

His expression turned serious as he said, "I do have a matter I must trouble Brother Wan with."

"Just call me Yuankai. Please speak!" Wan Yuankai waved his hands repeatedly.

`"My Li family has only recently arrived,` and we know nothing of the various families in the surrounding area. Would Brother Wan be willing to clear my doubts?"

"Naturally."

Wan Yuankai nodded. Seeing Li Yesheng fetch paper and a brush, he rolled up his sleeves and drew a thin line on the paper, explaining, "This is Guli Road."

Following this, he drew a circle in the middle of Guli Road and wrote the character for Li. Li Xiangping nodded, took a brush of his own, dipped it in ink, and drew a circle above and below the Li character, labeling them Moongaze Lake and the Great Li Mountains.

"My Li family has its back against the Great Li Mountains and faces Moongaze Lake, with `Guli Road cutting horizontally across.`"

Wan Yuankai nodded and drew a small circle to the right of the Li family, explaining, "To the east is my Wan family."

He then raised his brush and drew a large circle above the Wan family, speaking with utmost solemnity, "This is the Ji clan of the Tangjin Sect."

"The Ji clan of the Tangjin Sect?" `Li Xiangping's heart instantly tightened as he asked.`

"Indeed! This Tangjin Sect is a `cultivator sect` of Great Xu to the north, its strength on par with our Azure Pond Sect. Moongaze Lake is incredibly vast. If you cross the center of the lake and head north, you will reach the Tangjin Sect's mountain gate at Mount Tangdao. The Ji family is a power under the rule of this Tangjin Sect."

Nodding at Wan Yuankai to signal him to continue, Li Xiangping smiled bitterly to himself, _It really is a case of what you fear most coming to pass._

"For over a decade, there has been constant friction between the Tangjin Sect and the Azure Pond Sect. Although the two sects have yet to openly declare war, more than a dozen families along the border have been destroyed, leaving tens of thousands of mortals displaced. Both sides are merely testing each other."

Wan Yuankai's expression was uneasy, as if he had finally voiced a profound worry that had been weighing on his heart for a long time.

"This Ji family has also repeatedly struck out, invading my Wan family's territory and causing us untold suffering. The Ji family's patriarch, Ji Dengqi, achieved Qi Refinement a few years ago. My father has `only newly advanced` to the Jade Capital Wheel and is no match for him."

"Whenever our spirit rice ripens, those of the Ji family don masks and pretend to be rogue cultivators, coming into our village to pillage. For several years now, we have had no choice but to cower on the mountain and let the Ji family plunder the spirit rice."

Speaking up to this point, Wan Yuankai's eyes were slightly red, his fists clenched tightly.

Hearing these words, Li Xiangping had already guessed the purpose of Wan Yuankai's visit. His expression looked somewhat unsightly as he spoke, "My Li family's influence is weak and our strength is meager. I fear we cannot be of any help."

Seeing Wan Yuankai's grief and indignation, Li Xiangping hurriedly asked, "How many cultivators does the Wan family have in total?"

Wan Yuankai suddenly froze. He looked at Li Xiangping hesitantly, gritted his teeth, and said, "My father is at the peak of the Jade Capital Wheel, and I have just cultivated to the Azure Essence Wheel. There are two juniors in the family who have just reached the Profound Scenery Wheel. As for the Ji family, aside from that old thief who achieved Qi Refinement, there are two at the Heavenly Circuit Wheel and one at the Bearing Brightness Wheel. As for the Profound Scenery Wheel, there should be several more, but they never come along to pillage."

Upon hearing this, Li Xiangping asked with some confusion, "Someone within the sect once told me that if we encounter an enemy attack, we can use our jade seal to seek aid from the sect, and the sect will dispatch a Foundation Establishment cultivator to resolve your immediate crisis."

"How could we possibly move a Foundation Establishment cultivator!"

Wan Yuankai cried out sharply, gnashing his teeth, his fists clenched tight in an appearance of deep-seated hatred.

"If we activate the jade seal, it will take at least two hours for a Qi Refinement cultivator from the city to arrive. By then, it will be too late for anything. Not to mention the spirit rice being completely lost, but repeatedly requesting aid will unnecessarily offend the cultivator who comes to help!"

Having just finished speaking, and seeing Li Xiangping about to open his mouth again, Wan Yuankai hastily added, "Now that my father has sent me to visit everyone, it is also with the hope of forming an alliance between our two families, to borrow the strength of your esteemed clan..."

Li Xiangping sighed inwardly. Since Wan Yuankai had already broached the subject, he abandoned the thought of digging for more information and smiled bitterly.

"It is not that we are unwilling, but that we truly cannot! My Li family's lineage is thin. It was only years ago that we stepped through the gates of cultivation. Of my father's four sons, the eldest passed away early. Although my youngest brother's cultivation is high, he has already gone to cultivate in the sect. My elder brother and I both have shallow cultivation, even inferior to yours. What do we have to oppose the Ji family with?"

"Is there not still your esteemed father!" Wan Yuankai looked at Li Xiangping, his face full of anticipation as he spoke.

"To tell you the truth, my father is nothing but a mortal..."

"Impossible! How could a mere mortal sire three children with spiritual apertures? My father, with his Jade Capital Wheel cultivation, had a full seventeen children before he finally had me."

Wan Yuankai's face instantly filled with suspicion. Li Xiangping was momentarily horrified, his thoughts flashing as he followed Wan Yuankai's words with almost no hesitation, letting out a bitter smile, "In his early years, my father fought with someone and his cultivation was crippled..."

Wan Yuankai's expression immediately changed, and he answered with some shame, "I was reckless! Your esteemed father must have already cultivated the foundation of the Great Dao. It was Yuankai who was abrupt."

After saying this, he sat blankly back in his seat, his eyes full of sorrow.

On this side, Li Xiangping was also filled with trepidation. Wan Yuankai's sudden outburst just now had truly startled him, causing cold sweat to run down his back and nearly breaking his composure.

_So it turns out that the higher a cultivator's cultivation, the greater the probability of birthing a child with a spiritual aperture. I almost exposed a flaw in such an insignificant detail!_

Seeing Wan Yuankai's downcast expression, Li Xiangping calmed his state of mind and spoke, "Then, does Brother Wan know the situation at the end of Guli Road, to the west of my Li family?"

"West of Guli Road..."

Wan Yuankai was taken aback. He stared at the drawing thoughtfully, concentrating as he pondered, "The terrain of the Great Li Mountains slopes westward. As Guli Road heads west, it mostly enters the mountains gradually. I have never heard of any human habitation along that stretch, though I have heard..."

"Heard what?"

Li Xiangping quickly asked.

"I heard my father say that our ancestors explored to the west, and there have been sightings of the Mount Yue along the westward path of the Guli road."

# Chapter 30: Discussion

Having extracted every bit of information Wan Yuankai knew, Li Xiangping finally saw off the despondent Wan Yuankai. Carefully putting away the rough map in his hands, he took Li Yesheng and returned to Lijing.

Thanks to the continuous stream of field taxes delivered year after year from the various villages, the Li family was able to support more artisans. These people primarily built the small courtyards on the rear mountain, and when the construction schedule was loose, they were also responsible for repairing Lijing.

After more than half a year of renovations, Lijing had changed its appearance. Polished bluestone slabs were pieced together one by one, extending from the Li family's front door all the way to the village entrance. `As the main artery of Lijing, it broadcasted influence for this hub of power over the surrounding villages.`

The wealthy households in the village also began to imitate the Li family's blue-brick and tiled houses, remodeling their own adobe and thatched cottages. Liu Linfeng and the others did not dare to use blue bricks, opting instead for the slightly inferior white bricks, and then using plaster to outline the wall seams. Although not as striking or beautiful as blue bricks with white seams, it could still be considered grand.

Looking from afar toward the clusters of buildings behind the village, the white-brick courtyards surrounded the blue-brick and gray-tiled main residence. Amidst the ethereal wind and rain, it carried the flavor of a `southern river town`.

As Li Xiangping returned to the courtyard, he saw Li Mutian resting there with Li Xuanxuan. Li Mutian was full of vigor and agile in his movements. Holding a tea bowl in his hand, he sat calmly at the table, seemingly having already walked out from the pain of losing his son years ago.

Nowadays, Li Mutian did not manage many affairs, but no one dared to underestimate this old man. Although Li Mutian was merely a mortal, Li Yesheng and the others feared him far more than they did Li Tongya or Li Xiangping.

"Xuan'er!"

Li Xiangping smiled and caught the lunging Li Xuanxuan in his arms. The child had reached the age for schooling, and the Li family had hired Han Wenxu to be his primary teacher. Li Xuanxuan was bright and clever, making him effortless to teach.

"Today, Xie Wen and I went to the reed marshes. He said that Uncle Yesheng used to herd ducks there when he was little!"

Li Xuanxuan giggled, hugging Li Xiangping as he spoke in a childish voice.

The Li family had taken charge of arranging a marriage for Li Yesheng. Li Xiewen was Li Yesheng's child, just over two years old, who ran around after Li Xuanxuan every day.

"Isn't that the truth." Behind them, Li Yesheng answered with a smile.

"Call Second Brother back."

Li Xiangping turned his head and instructed with a smile. He watched Li Yesheng acknowledge the order and retreat, gradually walking away, before looking back at Li Xuanxuan.

"At that time, your Third Uncle was on the side catching fish. Those fish were so fat, covered in green scales, and incredibly slippery."

Li Xiangping set him down and pinched Li Xuanxuan's little face, but his gaze drifted, staring blankly into the distance as if he had fallen into some profound memory.

"That fish was the most delicious fish your Third Uncle has ever eaten. It was a fish from heaven, an immortal among fishes..."

"How is the Wan family?"

Li Mutian glanced down briefly, setting the clear tea in his hand onto the table, interrupting Li Xiangping.

"Their strength far exceeds my Li family's, with a long and deep-rooted history. They have a patriarch with Jade Capital Wheel cultivation, and moreover, there is that Wan Yuankai, who achieved Azure Essence at such a young age."

Li Xiangping patted Li Xuanxuan, letting the child go play by himself, and raised his head to reply.

Just as he was speaking, Li Tongya stepped into the courtyard. His expression was grave as he found a seat and listened carefully to Li Xiangping's explanation.

Li Xiangping recounted everything Wan Yuankai had said in one breath. Only then did he pick up his tea bowl, gulping down large mouthfuls while looking at the two of them.

"If this Wan family is truly beset from within and without, we should help them if we are able. The Ji family is right on our doorstep. If this Wan family really falls, without the lips, the teeth will grow cold; the next one will most likely be my Li family."

Li Tongya silently listened to Li Xiangping's words, holding up the rough map to examine it.

"The Wan family must be saved, but there must be a method to saving them. It must be a method that is most beneficial to my Li family. Xiangping, listen well."

Li Mutian narrowed his eyes, raised an eyebrow, and looked quietly at the tea in his cup before speaking again:

"The Wan family says the Ji family steals their spirit rice, so reply like this—tell them that my Li family still has vacant spirit fields, but no manpower. Tell him to send some people to my Li family to plant rice right here. My Li family will take thirty percent of the harvest. If Wan Yuankai truly sends people over, then his Wan family is indeed trapped by the Ji family and suffering unspeakably."

"Exactly."

Li Xiangping nodded, clearly sharing his father's line of thought, and said seriously, "It's just that this Ji family knows the Wan family's spirit fields like the back of their hand; their interior must have already become a sieve. This matter must be done in utmost secrecy by Wan Yuankai. Right now, my Li family absolutely cannot clash with the Ji family; quietly pushing and supporting the Wan family from behind will be enough."

"Not bad."

Li Mutian nodded in appreciation. He took a light sip of tea and continued, "The strength of both families far exceeds ours. Right now, we still need to conceal our capacities and bide our time, accumulating our strength. The Ji family is aggressively pushing forward, and their intentions are malicious. As for the Wan family, although they are also Under Azure Pond's Rule, they are sleeping beside our bed, and we must likewise keep our guard up against them."

Li Tongya stared at the map for a long while before suddenly speaking, "Does the Ji family on this map share a border with my Li family?"

"Yes."

Li Xiangping nodded, tapping a spot at Lidaokou, and said in a deep voice, "Guli Road diverges here. One path goes north, directly entering the Ji family's territory, while the other goes east, leading into the Wan family's territory."

He paused, and seeing Li Tongya deep in thought, Li Xiangping continued, "I asked Wan Yuankai. Over a hundred years ago, a demon gathered a few lesser demons and built a demon cave on that path leading to the Ji family. As a result, that path was cut off. The Ji family abandoned both the path and that piece of land, only managing it in name."

"So that's how it is," Li Tongya realized. "In that case, we need not fear the Ji family crossing our borders. As long as the Wan family does not fall, the Ji family absolutely cannot threaten this place."

"That demon is quite smart, too. It chose the border between the Azure Pond Sect and the Tangjin Sect, wholly focusing on cultivating inside its demon cave without coming out to cause trouble."

Li Xiangping spoke softly, turning his head to look at Li Mutian. "I heard from Wan Yuankai that the Mount Yue has been spotted to the west of our family. Father, when you fought with the Mount Yue back then, was there any news?"

Li Mutian waved his hand and said, "As for their appearance, those people of the Mount Yue have no difference from us, except they cut their hair and tattoo their bodies, and our languages do not communicate. Moreover, the Mount Yue love martial arts and are accustomed to battle, revering physical strength. Their tribe has those shamans who are skilled in curses and driving beasts, making them difficult to deal with."

"I don't know how effective your ⟨Evil Warding Art⟩ is. As long as we can handle those curses, the village militiamen and the enemy are about equal in strength, so it's not impossible to deal with them."

"There is no rush."

Li Tongya spoke up. His finger lightly drew a circle around the Li family on the map as he said, "The Wan family has suffered multiple squeezes and partitionings by the Ji family. The area Si Yuanbai demarcated for my Li family is also large. Today, my Li family's territory might even be larger than the Wan family's territory."

"Our land is vast, yet our people are scarce, and my Li family's strength is meager; we are merely swollen with fat, not truly robust. If the Mount Yue do not come, we have no need to provoke them. With the Wan family blocking in the front, let my Li family develop for a few years and thoroughly master these territories before discussing anything else."
