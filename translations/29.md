# Chapter 29: Seeking Aid

Li Xiangping was instantly startled. Seeing Wan Yuankai's beaming face, his thoughts raced, and he noted inwardly, _The Wan family is being a bit too enthusiastic. I need to keep my guard up._

His smile remaining unchanged, he cupped his hands toward Wan Yuankai, shaking his head repeatedly. "Your esteemed father is too polite! I absolutely cannot accept this!"

Wan Yuankai shook his head and retrieved a small pine wood box from his sleeve. Opening the polished lid, it revealed a pale green fruit resting on a cloth lining.

The fruit's surface was covered in a smooth, keratinous layer akin to snake scales. The interplay of pale green and white reflected a faint luminescence under the sunlight, giving it a truly remarkable appearance.

Wan Yuankai, however, wore a look of embarrassment as he whispered, "My Wan family is beset by difficulties both internal and external, spending more than we earn year after year. This Songyue fruit offers some minor benefits for shaping the Bearing Brightness Wheel, but such a trifle is hardly worth mentioning. I implore Brother Li to accept it, so I might return and report my mission accomplished."

Seeing Li Xiangping examining the fruit, Wan Yuankai suddenly realized something and hurriedly explained, "You simply slice the skin with a small knife and drink the juice within."

Li Xiangping stared at the fruit, his mind constantly weighing the situation.

He thought to himself, _If the Wan family is truly beset by difficulties inside and out, why tell me? Isn't that just inviting unwarranted greed?_

_Forget it! Regardless of what game he's playing, I should observe the situation first!_

"Then it would be impolite for me to refuse!"

Li Xiangping laughed heartily. He watched as Li Yesheng stepped forward to receive the box and carefully carry it away before shifting the conversation.

His expression turned serious as he said, "I do have a matter I must trouble Brother Wan with."

"Just call me Yuankai. Please speak!" Wan Yuankai waved his hands repeatedly.

"My Li family has only recently stepped into the world, and we know nothing of the various families in the surrounding area. Would Brother Wan be willing to clear my doubts?"

"Naturally."

Wan Yuankai nodded. Seeing Li Yesheng fetch paper and a brush, he rolled up his sleeves and drew a thin line on the paper, explaining, "This is Guli Road."

Following this, he drew a circle in the middle of Guli Road and wrote the character for Li. Li Xiangping nodded, took a brush of his own, dipped it in ink, and drew a circle above and below the Li character, labeling them Moongaze Lake and the Great Li Mountains.

"My Li family has its back against the Great Li Mountains and faces Moongaze Lake, with with Guli Road cutting straight through."

Wan Yuankai nodded and drew a small circle to the right of the Li family, explaining, "To the east is my Wan family."

He then raised his brush and drew a large circle above the Wan family, speaking with utmost solemnity, "This is the Ji clan of the Metal Tang Gate."

"The Ji clan of the Metal Tang Gate?" Li Xiangping's heart instantly tightened as he echoed the name.

"Indeed! This Metal Tang Gate is an immortal sect of Great Xu to the north, its strength on par with our Azure Pond Sect. Moongaze Lake is incredibly vast. If you cross the center of the lake and head north, you will reach the Metal Tang Gate's mountain gate at Mount Tangdao. The Ji family is a power under the rule of this Metal Tang Gate."

Nodding at Wan Yuankai to signal him to continue, Li Xiangping smiled bitterly to himself, _It really is a case of what you fear most coming to pass._

"For over a decade, there has been constant friction between the Metal Tang Gate and the Azure Pond Sect. Although the two sects have yet to openly declare war, more than a dozen families along the border have been destroyed, leaving tens of thousands of mortals displaced. Both sides are merely testing each other."

Wan Yuankai's expression was uneasy, as if he had finally voiced a profound worry that had been weighing on his heart for a long time.

"This Ji family has also repeatedly struck out, invading my Wan family's territory and causing us untold suffering. The Ji family's patriarch, Ji Dengqi, achieved Qi Refinement a few years ago. My father has only recently advanced to the Jade Capital Wheel..."

"Whenever our spirit rice ripens, those of the Ji family don masks and pretend to be rogue cultivators, coming into our village to pillage. For several years now, we have had no choice but to cower on the mountain and let the Ji family plunder the spirit rice."

Speaking up to this point, Wan Yuankai's eyes were slightly red, his fists clenched tightly.

Hearing these words, Li Xiangping had already guessed the purpose of Wan Yuankai's visit. His expression looked somewhat unsightly as he spoke, "My Li family's influence is weak and our strength is meager. I fear we cannot be of any help."

Seeing Wan Yuankai's grief and indignation, Li Xiangping hurriedly asked, "How many cultivators does the Wan family have in total?"

Wan Yuankai suddenly froze. He looked at Li Xiangping hesitantly, gritted his teeth, and said, "My father is at the peak of the Jade Capital Wheel, and I have just cultivated to the Azure Essence Wheel. There are two juniors in the family who have just reached the Profound Scenery Wheel. As for the Ji family, aside from that old thief who achieved Qi Refinement, there are two at the Heavenly Circuit Wheel and one at the Bearing Brightness Wheel. As for the Profound Scenery Wheel, there should be several more, but they never come along to pillage."

Upon hearing this, Li Xiangping asked with some confusion, "Someone within the sect once told me that if we encounter an enemy attack, we can use our jade seal to seek aid from the sect, and the sect will dispatch a Foundation Establishment cultivator to resolve your immediate crisis."

"How could we possibly move a Foundation Establishment cultivator!"

Wan Yuankai cried out sharply, gnashing his teeth, his fists clenched tight in an appearance of deep-seated hatred.

"If we activate the jade seal, it will take at least two hours for a Qi Refinement cultivator from the city to arrive. By then, it will be too late for anything. Not to mention the spirit rice being completely lost, but repeatedly requesting aid will unnecessarily offend the cultivator who comes to help!"

Having just finished speaking, and seeing Li Xiangping about to open his mouth again, Wan Yuankai hastily added, "Now that my father has sent me to visit everyone, it is also with the hope of forming an alliance between our two families, to borrow the strength of your esteemed clan..."

Li Xiangping sighed inwardly. Since Wan Yuankai had already broached the subject, he abandoned the thought of digging for more information and smiled bitterly.

"It is not that we are unwilling, but that we truly cannot! My Li family's lineage is thin. It was only years ago that we stepped through the gates of cultivation. Of my father's four sons, the eldest passed away early. Although my youngest brother's cultivation is high, he has already gone to cultivate in the sect. My elder brother and I both have shallow cultivation, even inferior to yours. What do we have to oppose the Ji family with?"

"Is there not still your esteemed father!" Wan Yuankai looked at Li Xiangping, his face full of anticipation as he spoke.

"To tell you the truth, my father is nothing but a mortal..."

"Impossible! How could a mere mortal sire three children with spiritual apertures? My father, with his Jade Capital Wheel cultivation, had a full seventeen children before he finally had me."

Wan Yuankai's face instantly filled with suspicion. Li Xiangping was momentarily horrified, his thoughts flashing as he followed Wan Yuankai's words with almost no hesitation, letting out a bitter smile, "In his early years, my father fought with someone and his cultivation was crippled..."

Wan Yuankai's expression immediately changed, and he answered with some shame, "I was reckless! Your esteemed father must have already cultivated the foundation of the Great Dao. It was Yuankai who was abrupt."

After saying this, he sat blankly back in his seat, his eyes full of sorrow.

On this side, Li Xiangping was also filled with trepidation. Wan Yuankai's sudden outburst just now had truly startled him, causing cold sweat to run down his back and nearly breaking his composure.

_So it turns out that the higher a cultivator's cultivation, the greater the probability of birthing a child with a spiritual aperture. I almost exposed a flaw in such an insignificant detail!_

Seeing Wan Yuankai's downcast expression, Li Xiangping calmed his state of mind and spoke, "Then, does Brother Wan know the situation at the end of Guli Road, to the west of my Li family?"

"West of Guli Road..."

Wan Yuankai was taken aback. He stared at the drawing thoughtfully, concentrating as he pondered, "The terrain of the Great Li Mountains slopes westward. As Guli Road heads west, it mostly enters the mountains gradually. I have never heard of any human habitation along that stretch, though I have heard..."

"Heard what?"

Li Xiangping quickly asked.

"I heard my father say that our ancestors explored to the west, and there have been sightings of the Mount Yue along the westward path of the Guli road."

"Mount Yue?"
