# Chapter 11: Summer Solstice

A drop of rain on the Summer Solstice is worth a thousand pieces of gold.

Hazy raindrops struck the fields. The bare earth emanated the rich scent of soil. In the south, wheat ripens early. The newly harvested wheat had already been gathered, tied into bundles, and stacked across the fields.

Farmers greeted one another along the field ridges. Seeds for oil-plants, turnips, and mustard greens were ready to be sown, awaiting the late autumn harvest.

At the Li family residence, Li Tongya, Li Changhu, and the others had longed for the stars and the moon, counting the days on their fingers, until they finally welcomed the arrival of the Summer Solstice.

As the sky just began to turn a dusky gray, the two brought out the altar table and knelt before it alongside Li Xiangping.

The dharma mirror was propped up by the hornless dragon mirror stand. Upon the altar lay the first stalks of new wheat harvested during the solstice, assorted fruits, and three cups of clear tea brewed with summer rain.

The three exchanged a glance and spoke in unison:

"I, disciple Li Changhu/Li Tongya/Li Xiangping of the Li family, respectfully request the Profound Brilliance Wondrous Dharma, to govern my destiny and soothe my spirit, as I dedicate myself to pursuing the Dao."

"I shall perform meritorious deeds at the proper times, failing not in my efficacy and faith. As the talisman burns, my body offers its gratitude to the Supreme Yin."

Brilliant colors swirled upon the dharma mirror, its white light flashing continuously. Three white pills abruptly leaped from its surface and flew toward the three of them.

The three hurriedly sat cross-legged, guiding the talisman seeds and receiving the incantations according to the techniques in the ⟨Reception Method⟩.

Watching the white pills fall into the Niwan Palaces of the three, Li Chejing withdrew his gaze. He sat cross-legged, sinking his breath to enter a state of meditative stillness.

Gazing at the pure white expanse of lunar essence within his Qihai aperture, he thought, _Relying entirely on the dharma mirror's refinement, I have already forged eighty-one wisps of lunar essence. On the day of the Summer Solstice, the heavenly qi proclaims life, and the vibrant yang dispels the dark. It is the perfect opportunity to break through!_

Like a newborn calf unafraid of tigers, Li Chejing shifted his intent and formed hand seals. Waves surged within his Qihai aperture as the eighty-one wisps of lunar essence spiritual qi rapidly gathered, chasing each other and swimming head to tail.

_Entering the crown of the Niwan Palace to merge as one, it descends the twelve rings of the Twelve-Story Pagoda... thus manifesting a soaring vitality. The vital qi curls gracefully, ascending the twelve rings of the Twelve-Story Pagoda, rising from the aperture beneath the tongue..._

Li Chejing silently recited the method for condensing the Profound Scenery Wheel from the ⟨Supreme Yin Breathing Wheel-Nurturing Scripture⟩. The lunar essence vaulted over the Twelve-Story Pagoda, gathering in the Niwan Palace where it condensed into liquid, before flowing steadily downward to pool into a clear pond within his Qihai aperture.

"Rise!"

He called out softly. A bright silver arc of light emerged from the clear pond like the moon's reflection in water. This arc was crystal clear, resembling an exceptionally fine crescent moon. It was breathtakingly beautiful.

"The Twelve-Story Pagoda flows into the Qihai; from the clear pond arises the Profound Scenery Wheel."

Gazing at this sight, Li Chejing could not help but voice his admiration. He immediately channeled the incantation to solidify the Profound Scenery Wheel, doing his utmost to bind its form.

Yet, as time slowly trickled by, the Profound Scenery Wheel hesitated to truly settle. The crystal-clear crescent drifted gently within the pond, its light rippling and floating erratically, appearing as though it might dissipate at any moment.

By now, Li Chejing was utterly exhausted. Struggling to maintain the form of the Profound Scenery Wheel, large beads of sweat rolled down his handsome face, soaking his pale green robes.

Caught in this dilemma, the clear pond in his Qihai aperture suddenly parted with a splash, and a white talisman pill abruptly flew upward.

The perfectly round and brilliantly luminous talisman pill affixed itself onto the Profound Scenery Wheel. Instantly, the translucent crescent stabilized its form, and the dharma power within the clear pond gradually steadied. Li Chejing let out a long breath and circulated his qi to conclude his cultivation.

With the Profound Scenery Wheel successfully formed, the lunar essence spiritual qi within his body transformed into lunar essence dharma power. Li Chejing had officially become an immortal cultivator in the Fetal Breathing stage!

He slowly opened his eyes, looked toward the dharma mirror faintly discernible within the lunar halo on the altar, and bowed deeply.

"For bestowing immortal arts, refining the lunar essence, and the successful formation of the Profound Scenery Wheel, the Li family is boundlessly grateful for this grace. From this day forth, year after year, month after month, the incense shall burn continuously, and the sacrifices shall never cease."

Li Chejing's still somewhat youthful voice was extraordinarily solemn. This boy, who had always been sheltered beneath his brothers' wings, had remarkably grown into a young man.

"Jing'er."

Li Mutian stood quietly beneath the moonlight, looking gently at his youngest son.

"Father, I have condensed the Profound Scenery Wheel."

Li Chejing stood up and smiled at his father. He gently opened his left hand, and a milky-white stream of dharma power surfaced in his palm.

"The first step of cultivation is called Fetal Breathing. The Fetal Breathing stage requires the condensation of the Six Wheels, which are respectively named Profound Scenery, Bearing Brightness, Heavenly Circuit, Azure Essence, Jade Capital, and Nascent Spirit. This Profound Scenery Wheel is the gateway to the Dao."

"Good, good," Li Mutian said repeatedly, finding great comfort in his old age.

Waving his hand to dispel the dharma power, Li Chejing sat cross-legged once more to consolidate his cultivation base.

"What a fine incantation!"

Li Xiangping was the first to open his eyes. With a smile, he stretched lazily, stood up, grabbed the teapot from the table, and took a massive gulp.

"I received the wheel-nurturing scripture and an incantation known as the ⟨Water Repelling Method⟩."

As soon as his voice fell, Li Tongya awoke from his meditation. He raised an eyebrow and said contentedly, "The incantation I received is known as the ⟨Evil Warding Art⟩."

The two chatted for a short while before Li Changhu finally opened his eyes.

Seeing his three younger brothers staring at him eagerly, he said helplessly, "The incantation is called the ⟨Cleanse Clothing Art⟩."

Now that all three older brothers were awake, Li Chejing, who had been holding back for a long time, finally spoke with excitement, "I have already condensed the Profound Scenery Wheel!"

"So fast?" Li Xiangping exclaimed in amazement, tilting his head in slight confusion.

"It is entirely due to the power of the dharma mirror." Li Chejing looked at the dharma mirror on the altar and spoke sincerely, "This mirror gathers lunar essence on its own. Using it to temper lunar essence power truly yields twice the result with half the effort."

Only then did Li Tongya and the others realize the truth. Li Changhu rubbed Li Chejing's head and said with a smile, "I have heard from books that the human body possesses spiritual apertures, and one can find their locations when dharma power courses through the body. Jing'er, perhaps you can check for us."

Li Changhu felt that his own reception of the talisman seed had been incredibly strenuous, far less smooth than his two brothers. He was secretly beginning to doubt his own natural talent for immortal cultivation.

Sweeping his gaze over his three older brothers, Li Chejing placed his left hand on the shoulder of the closest one, Li Xiangping, and closed his eyes to search for a spiritual aperture.

_The qi surges upward, pulsating ceaselessly like a leaping fish..._

Li Chejing silently recalled the descriptions from the breathing method, sending his dharma power circling through Li Xiangping's meridians.

After about the time it takes an incense stick to burn, he opened his eyes in shock and muttered to himself, "None..."

Stunned, he hurriedly placed his hands on the shoulders of his other two brothers, probing with his dharma power.

"None... how can there be none?!"

Li Chejing's face turned somewhat pale. He sat cross-legged where he was, closed his eyes to enter meditation, and circulated his dharma power through his own body. Sure enough, there was no so-called spiritual aperture.

"The ⟨Supreme Yin Breathing Wheel-Nurturing Scripture⟩ clearly states that the spiritual aperture is the foundation of the Great Dao. Regardless of the immortal incantation or wondrous dharma, without a spiritual aperture, cultivation is impossible..."

"This... this..."

Li Chejing's state of mind fluctuated endlessly, to the point where he could not even maintain his meditative state. With trembling lips, he looked at Li Mutian, who was fiddling with his tea leaves, and said softly, "Father!"

"You were right. This dharma mirror is indeed a calamity that could annihilate our entire family."

"If true cultivators learn of this item, not only will our bodies perish and our souls be detained in the hands of others, but the entirety of Lijing Village, and even every large and small settlement along Guli Road, will be reduced to ash."

"I have already formed the Profound Scenery Wheel, and my dharma power flows throughout my body. Unless I cripple my own cultivation base, those Qi Refinement cultivators will be unable to use their dharma power to probe for the presence of my spiritual aperture."

Li Chejing stood up abruptly, his expression fraught with terror, and solemnly warned, "As for our Li family disciples, those who have received the profound pearl talisman seed but have not yet formed the Profound Scenery Wheel absolutely must not parade themselves in public!"

"Exactly so."

Li Mutian took a sip of his tea, looking at them with a grave expression.

"When Jing'er was initially able to cultivate, I simply assumed he possessed extraordinary natural talent. Han Wenxu once said that those possessing a spiritual aperture are one in a thousand miles; perhaps Jing'er was that one."

He let out a self-deprecating laugh and continued, "Looking at it now, this dharma mirror is truly an incredible immortal artifact. It can turn the rotten into the miraculous. By bestowing these talisman seeds, even mortals can catch a glimpse of the immortal Dao."

The brothers felt a mix of immense relief and profound terror, silently watching their father.

"Enough. Go to your cultivation. I am going to check on the fields," Li Mutian said after a long silence.

"Yes."

The brothers naturally bowed their heads in compliance, returning to their respective rooms to cultivate.

Li Mutian, meanwhile, reverently put away the dharma mirror, tidied the altar table, and headed to the fields with his hands clasped behind his back.

**Translator's Note**

The sequence Li Chejing undergoes to form his Profound Scenery Wheel is adapted from actual Daoist internal alchemy (Neidan), specifically the completetion of the "Microcosmic Orbit" (小周天 - Xiao Zhou Tian).

The process begins in the _Qihai_ (Sea of Qi / Lower Dantian). In Daoist terminology, "Fetal Breathing" does not mean using the lungs. Instead, it refers to establishing an internal, self-sustaining rhythm of Qi that mimics how a fetus "breathes" in the womb—pure, innate, and entirely internal.

The refined lunar essence is pushed upward from the abdoment, traveling up the spine (along the Governing Vessel) until it reaches the top of the head.

The Qi enters the Niwan Palace (the Upper Dantian, located within the brain). Here, the ethereal, gaseous spiritual qi merges and condenses into a heavier, liquid state.

To complete the circuit, the cultivator presses their tongue against the roof of their mouth, bridging the body's upper and lower energy vessels. The liquid Qi drops from the "aperture beneath the tongue," flowing straight down the throat/windpipe—termed the Twelve-Story Pagoda—to return to the abdomen.

As this highly refined, liquid Qi cascades back into the Qihai, it pools to form a "clear pond." The Profound Scenery Wheel—manifesting as a glowing, crystal-clear silver crescent—is the physical crystallization of this cycled energy. Its formation signifies that Li Chejing has permanently opened his body's internal circuitry and successfully established the foundation of his Dao.
