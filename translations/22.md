# Chapter 22: Immortal Sect

Li Tongya and the others sat properly in the main courtyard of the Li Residence. Down below, Li Yesheng stood in the courtyard, reading the charcoal characters on the cloth aloud.

"Lijing has two hundred and twenty-nine households; Lichuankou, one hundred and one; Jingyang, two hundred and ninety-seven; and Lidaokou, one hundred and fifty-eight. This totals seven hundred and eighty-five households, comprising four thousand two hundred and eighty people."

"Four thousand two hundred and eighty people... with roughly three able-bodied individuals per household, we should be able to scrape together one or two who possess a spiritual aperture," Li Xiangping muttered in a low voice, leaning against the wooden table.

"The various clan elders of the Ye family came to visit Yesheng last night... hoping to restore their ancestral surname," Li Yesheng reported with a troubled smile on his face.

"Ancestral surname?" Li Tongya asked, looking up with a puzzled expression.

Seeing the puzzled expressions on Li Tongya and the others, Li Mutian coughed and spoke up to explain, "The ancestors of the Ye clan were originally a branch of our Li family. However, they later committed an offense, and that entire branch was expelled from the Li family and changed their surname to Ye."

"Originally, neither family mentioned this matter. Now, seeing that our Li family has encountered immortal destiny, the Ye clan is naturally eager to latch onto us and wishes to change back to their ancestral surname."

Hearing this, Li Yesheng nodded as well. He had only learned of this old, hidden secret last night while listening to the clan elder's tearful pleas. That Ye clan elder had wept so bitterly and shed so many tears that, if not for the fact he only came to curry favor right after the Li family obtained immortal destiny, Li Yesheng might have actually believed the man was devoted to the main family.

"If any branch of the Ye family produces someone with a spiritual aperture, then permit that branch to change back to the Li surname and serve as a collateral branch of our Li family, " Having heard Li Mutian's explanation, Li Xiangping immediately answered Li Yesheng, instructing him to relay the reply to that clan elder.

He then turned his head to ask Li Mutian, "Father, about the managers for these villages..."

"Chen Erniu is shrewd and capable, and he fled here from Lichuankou originally, so we shall send him to Lichuankou. Jingyang has the largest population and is the wealthiest, so we must send someone close to our hearts; let your Uncle Ren oversee it."

Li Mutian took a light sip of tea and continued, "Lidaokou neighbors Guli Road and has a massive influx of fleeing refugees, making its situation the most complex. That Xu Wenshan is sly as a ghost; let him take his group of refugee brothers and go there."

"Father has thought of everything," Li Xiangping nodded, holding and reading the scroll detailing the methods for cultivating spiritual plants, "Our most pressing task is still to use the ⟨Spirit-Seeking Art⟩ within this scroll to survey the earth veins of these villages. That way, we can locate the spiritual fields and plant the spirit rice and White Essence Fruit as soon as possible."

---

Standing upon that flying shuttle, Li Chejing watched the vast earth beneath him. Houses small as ants and majestic cities all vanished into the distance. The sorrow of leaving home was instantly cast to the back of his mind, leaving his heart surging with boundless excitement.

_A true man should ride the wind, grasp the moon, and traverse the world with a sword in hand!_

After flying for about two hours, an extremely tall mountain range loomed before Li Chejing's eyes. Pavilions, terraces, and towers dotted the mountains in an endless, unbroken expanse, presenting the grand atmosphere of an immortal sect.

Flocks of immortal cranes and white birds wove between the peaks. Before him, Si Yuanbai ceased his meditation. With a wave of his hand, he tossed out a token and waited quietly.

"So it is Martial Uncle Yuanbai returning," a voice called out as a white crane slowly came to a halt before Li Chejing.

Catching the token in its beak, it asked curiously, "Who is this kid?"

It was Li Chejing's first time seeing a talking immortal beast, and he was so nervous he could not speak.

Si Yuanbai smiled and said, "A newly accepted disciple of my Qingsui Peak."

"Congratulations, Martial Uncle."

The immortal crane congratulated. After speaking, it gently flapped its wings, and a transparent barrier of light slowly opened a gap before the two of them.

Only then did Li Chejing realize there was a nearly transparent, massive dome stretching between heaven and earth, firmly encasing the mountain gate of the Azure Pond Sect and isolating the spiritual energy inside from the outside.

Upon entering the mountain gate of the Azure Pond Sect, a wave of refreshing comfort instantly washed over Li Chejing's body.

"Such a high concentration of spiritual energy."

Seeing Li Chejing's face full of astonishment, Si Yuanbai chuckled lightly, "This is the Azure Pond Sect's Heavenly Essence Unified Spiritual Array. It is incomparably wondrous, sealing the inside from the outside so that the Azure Pond Mountains' spiritual veins are enjoyed exclusively by the Azure Pond Sect. By relying on the array's power, as long as an immortal cultivator at the Foundation Establishment realm oversees it, even a Purple Mansion cultivator would find it difficult to breach."

"How truly wondrous," Li Chejing echoed, while secretly thinking in his heart, _I wonder if this array formation can be learned. I should set one up in our rear mountain as well._

Seeing Li Chejing's dazed expression, Si Yuanbai simply assumed he was awestruck and continued, "The hundred arts of immortal cultivation—array formations, pill concoction, dharma artifacts, talismans, beast taming, vein surveying, and so on—hold boundless mysteries. It is best if you can choose one to study."

"Master, which of the hundred arts of immortal cultivation do you practice?" Li Chejing asked curiously.

"Among the thirty-six peaks of Azure Pond, Qingsui Peak has always been renowned for its sword arts..." Si Yuanbai lifted his head proudly and replied.

"Oh, so that's how it is." Li Chejing nodded in sudden realization, looking toward the precious sword at Si Yuanbai's waist.

"Therefore... your master practices the Dao of Talismans."

Si Yuanbai looking at the dumbfounded Li Chejing, continued with some embarrassment, "Your grandmaster died early, so your master had yet to grasp the path of the sword immortal. However, Qingsui Peak constantly needed subsidies to maintain household expenses, so I had no choice but to learn some talisman crafting first. Who knew that the more I studied talismans, the more proficient I would become..."

"Oh, oh." Li Chejing nodded repeatedly to show his understanding.

"First, I will take you to meet a few of your martial brothers."

Si Yuanbai led Li Chejing weaving between the various peaks until they found the relatively low-set Qingsui Peak. As they landed on the peak's summit, a man and a woman walked over to greet them.

"You rank seventh under my tutelage. This is your Third Martial Brother, named Xiao Yuansi, at the seventh level of Qi Condensation. And that is your Fourth Martial Sister, named Yuan Tuan, at the third level of Qi Condensation."

Li Chejing bowed respectfully to the man and woman. Xiao Yuansi possessed thick eyebrows and large eyes, appearing quite dashing and generous.

He handed over a jade slip and said, "This is a scroll of sword techniques I acquired by chance while out. I give it to you now, Junior Martial Brother, as a greeting gift."

Yuan Tuan also smiled faintly. Presenting a jade pendant, she said, "This jade pendant can calm the spirit and steady the mind, aiding one in entering meditation. It is an excellent tool for the Fetal Breathing realm, and I gift it to you as well, Junior Martial Brother."

Li Chejing naturally thanked them one by one. Out of curiosity, he inquired, "Are our other martial brothers and sisters currently in secluded cultivation?"

Si Yuanbai smiled softly and replied, "Your Eldest Martial Brother had extraordinary innate talent. He cultivated to the grand perfection of Qi Condensation last year. He then went into secluded cultivation to break through to Foundation Establishment, but he failed, perishing and his Dao vanishing."

Si Yuanbai's answer clearly exceeded Li Chejing's expectations. He let out a low "Ah," gazing at Si Yuanbai with a complex expression.

"As for your other martial brothers and sisters, some died by the hands of demons, some died fighting over treasures, and others died facing bottlenecks and inner demons. They are all buried here on the peak. Come with me to pay respects to them."

Seeing Li Chejing's sorrowful face, Si Yuanbai sneered coldly and said, "The path of immortal cultivation is fundamentally a path of great contention! You must fight, and you must fight to win! If you cannot win the fight, you die—what is there to pity!"

"You, Li Chejing, must fight; if you cannot win, your body will perish. Your Li family must fight; if they cannot win, the clan will be exterminated. My Qingsui Peak must also fight; if we cannot win, our Dao will be severed. This world fundamentally operates on the strong preying on the weak—what is there to hide!"
