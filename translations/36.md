# Chapter 36: Why Bother

Liu Miaotu had wandered around the mountain for a day and a night. Let alone immortal treasures, he hadn't even seen a single hair.

Furious, he sat on a large bluestone and cursed the village idlers, "A bunch of plague-ridden idle dogs! Saying there were good treasures on the mountain—they were spouting pure nonsense!"

Seeing the mist rising in the mountains, Liu Miaotu shivered and thought inwardly, _Though the wolves, tigers, and leopards on this mountain were driven out a few years ago, who knows if any slipped through the net! This mist is eerie; I had better head down the mountain first..._

Liu Miaotu stood up and walked along the mountain path for nearly two hours, but there was only a hazy expanse of white mist, with no exit in sight.

"Truly eerie! Why am I still not down the mountain!"

Looking again at the large bluestone before him, a chill ran down Liu Miaotu's spine. He cried out in disbelief, "Impossible!"

This was clearly the very same large bluestone he had been sitting on earlier. He had walked along the mountain path for two hours; how could he still be in the exact same spot!

Frightened out of his wits, Liu Miaotu turned and ran. He sprinted for a distance while screaming, only stopping to catch his breath when he realized that the large bluestone had somehow appeared right in front of him again.

"Damn it... I can't get out."

Night had fallen. Liu Miaotu was dressed thinly. Under severe emotional distress, the cold wind blew over him, and he simply fainted.

At the break of dawn, Liu Miaotu finally woke up. His entire body was burning hot. He faintly heard shouting. Looking up, he vaguely saw a figure sitting not too far away.

Just as he climbed to his feet, he heard a loud shout, "Who is there!"

Liu Miaotu was already dizzy and disoriented; his knees instantly gave out and his feet slipped. He tumbled and rolled right to the front of that person.

Liu Linfeng took a look at the man's face and saw that it was actually a loafer from his own Liu family. In an instant, he understood everything. A surge of fury shot straight from his spleen to the crown of his head. 

His face flushed beet red as he bellowed, "Why the hell are you here?!"

Startled fully awake by the shout, Liu Miaotu cowered and answered, "I... I came up the mountain to chop wood."

"Bullshit!" Liu Linfeng cursed aloud. 

Pointing directly at Liu Miaotu's nose, he continued to berate him, "If you came up this mountain, why didn't you inform the main family? Is this a place the likes of you can come?"

Just as he finished cursing, Liu Linfeng himself jolted awake to reality. 

A chill crept up from his spine to the back of his head as he pondered inwardly, _How did my Liu family produce such a piece of trash? Now we are truly in deep trouble. My nephew is ruthless and paranoid; there's no way he will leave this man alive. I just pray this doesn't implicate my Liu family!_

Looking at Liu Miaotu kowtowing frantically, his head striking the ground like a pestle pounding garlic, Liu Linfeng formulated a plan.

Liu Linfeng's expression suddenly shifted. He said coldly, "Follow me. At the very least, I'll save your wretched life!"

Liu Miaotu hastily followed him into the courtyard. He then saw Liu Linfeng bring out a thick hemp rope, force Liu Miaotu's hands behind his back, and begin tying them up.

"Patriarch, you... what are you doing..." Liu Miaotu asked, cowering, his heart lacking any confidence.

"Taking you to plead for forgiveness. Only in this way will you show sincerity."

Liu Linfeng waved his hand, discreetly strapping a knife to his waist, and said coldly, "Move!"

"Yes, yes, yes! Thank you, Patriarch!"

Before long, the two arrived in front of the main courtyard. Liu Linfeng pointed to the muddy ground by the road and said, "Kneel there."

With that, he went to request an audience with Li Tongya and Li Xiangping. Entering the courtyard, he looked at the two men seated above, knelt down, and said bitterly, "The Liu family has been lax in its discipline. I beg the main family for forgiveness!"

This sudden kneeling frightened the two brothers, causing them to jump up from their seats.

They hurried over to help him up.

Li Tongya said in a deep voice, "You absolutely must not! How did it come to this!"

Liu Linfeng smiled bitterly and recounted Liu Miaotu's bastardly deeds. 

Upon hearing this, Li Xiangping merely said softly, "You may handle it however you see fit. There is no need for such grand formalities."

"Please come with me," Liu Linfeng shook his head and replied. He led the two to the gate and pointed at Liu Miaotu kneeling before them.

Seeing the three men, Liu Miaotu immediately began weeping and complaining. Little did he expect Liu Linfeng to spring forward and, in a single blur of motion, draw a blade across his throat.

Liu Miaotu only managed to squeak twice before blood sprayed like water from his neck. He coughed violently for a moment, spitting out over a dozen bloody bubbles, before collapsing to the ground with a heavy thud, never to rise again.

"Uncle is still meticulous." Li Xiangping clapped his hands, watching Liu Miaotu fall.

He then smiled at Li Tongya, "He chose a patch of mud to avoid dirtying our stone steps."

Li Tongya only let out a long sigh. Looking at Liu Linfeng, he said, "I will leave the disposal to you, Uncle."

"Naturally." Liu Linfeng quickly replied, dragging the corpse into the woods.

The two then turned and walked back into the courtyard. Li Tongya maintained a deep frown, but Li Xiangping patted his shoulder and said in a deep voice, "He brought it upon himself."

Li Tongya smiled bitterly. Meeting his younger brother's eyes, he explained, "I am not pitying him; I am merely reflecting. It is exactly because you are here to manage the Li family that Father is willing to let go and not interfere. The rest of us are ultimately not ruthless enough."

Li Xiangping chuckled and shook his head in reply. "You, Li Tongya, would also kill him. It's just that you would summon the village to bear witness, enumerate his eight great crimes, and publicly execute him—all to make yourself feel better."

"If the result is killing a man either way, why bother!"

---

Standing before the small courtyard at the foot of the mountain, Li Xiangping's words echoed in Li Tongya's mind, filling him with deep emotion.

_If I do not kill him, our Li family will become the next Wan family, infiltrated through and through until we are as full of holes as a sieve._

Shaking his head to toss the minor episode from his mind, Li Tongya gently pushed open the courtyard gate. He saw Liu Rouxuan obediently sitting cross-legged in cultivation, breathing and regulating spiritual qi.

_This ⟨Azure Essence Wheel-Nurturing Method⟩ relies purely on breathing spiritual qi to condense the Profound Scenery Wheel. Furthermore, its efficiency in refining that energy is abysmally low. No wonder Rouxuan is still a mortal despite cultivating for nearly two years. On the other hand, I heard Li Qiuyang, relying on the power of the Serpent Dragon Fruit, has progressed with divine speed and is about to condense the Profound Scenery Wheel._

Usually, Liu Rouxuan was always smiling whenever she saw him. Rarely did he catch her in such earnest cultivation. Li Tongya couldn't help but steal a few more glances. Noticing her eyelashes flutter and her nostrils flare gently—a sign she was about to awaken from meditation—he hurriedly shifted his gaze away.

"Brother Ya!" Opening her eyes and seeing Li Tongya, Liu Rouxuan immediately cried out in pleasant surprise.

"Mm." Lowering his eyes, Li Tongya gave a soft hum of acknowledgment, feeling somewhat like a thief with a guilty conscience.

Liu Rouxuan quietly observed his profile. Suddenly, she spoke up in a somewhat downcast tone, "I am already seventeen. My childhood friends in the village have probably long since married and had children, yet I don't even have a companion."

Hearing this, Li Tongya couldn't help but shake his head. He said softly, "Since you have already entered the immortal path, there is no need to dwell on such matters. You are different from them. Focus on your cultivation; condensing the Profound Scenery Wheel is your top priority."

"I have been very serious. I cultivate for sixteen hours a day!" Liu Rouxuan pouted and muttered in her defense. Then, looking up at Li Tongya, she asked, "Is Brother Ya also remaining single because of cultivation?"

Seeing Li Tongya nod slightly, Liu Rouxuan's face flushed red. With slightly rapid breathing, she asked in a whisper, "Brother Tongya... could you ever... find me worthy?"
