# Chapter 27: Changfu

Tonight, Lijing was brightly lit. Laughter and cheerful voices echoed from every household. Children ran about the village, playing and chasing one another as if it were the New Year festival.

Liu Linfeng led the villagers in building a roaring bonfire in the open square. Everyone gathered around the flames, chatting and laughing as they discussed that ferocious giant serpent. The men boasted proudly, their faces glowing red in the firelight.

At dusk, it had taken Li Yesheng a full ten men to drag the serpent down the mountain. After painstakingly stripping away its scales, the village women were called upon to carefully slice the snake meat. It filled three large wooden basins, marinated with scallions, ginger, and cooking wine, and placed beside the bonfire. Each family brought their own meat and vegetables, chatting merrily by the fire.

Li Mutian had never cared for such lively gatherings and could not be bothered to step out of his courtyard. Thus, Li Xiangping and Li Tongya instructed some men to guard the spirit root before heading down the mountain themselves.

Li Xiangping sat before the bonfire, turning his head to speak to his older brother, "Our knowledge is shallow, and we do not recognize this spirit root. Since we remain ignorant of its effects and taboos, it is best we leave it be for now. Another day, we shall find a market to discreetly inquire about it."

"Naturally." Li Tongya nodded. Glancing up at the three wooden basins, he picked up a slice of snake meat with his fingers. Seeing it cut as thin as a cicada's wing, seemingly translucent with a white gleam, he smiled.

"The ⟦Secret Missive of Lixia⟧ states: 'When scaled beasts, birds, and insects cultivate into demonic beasts, they inhale and exhale the pure essence of the sun and moon. Spiritual qi breeds within their flesh and blood. Consuming it fortifies the body and enriches one's vitality.' We should let Father eat more of this."

"It has already been sent to his courtyard."

Li Xiangping skewered a piece of meat, letting it sizzle over the fire as he continued.

"The snake gall was the size of two fists. It was also sent to the courtyard to be steeped in wine."

As the brothers conversed, Li Yesheng approached with Xu Wenshan in tow. Li Tongya looked up and raised an eyebrow, gesturing for them to speak their minds.

---

"You wish to establish a trade route along Guli Road, selling the surplus goods from the various villages?"

After listening to Xu Wenshan's earnest proposal, Li Xiangping and Li Tongya exchanged a glance, deep in thought. What Xu Wenshan planned coincided perfectly with their own intentions.

Li Tongya pondered for a few breaths before speaking, "Very well. You and Liu Linfeng shall take joint responsibility for this matter. Aside from your own crew, the remaining manpower will be drawn from the Liu family for now."

Seeing Xu Wenshan overjoyed, Li Xiangping added in a deep voice, "There are still a few cultivator clans along Guli Road. If we are to open trade, we must inevitably consult them. Send your men to make careful, probing contact first. Mark down all populated areas along the road and draft a map before anything else."

"This subordinate understands!"

Having listened carefully, Xu Wenshan raised his head and patted his chest, offering a firm guarantee.

"Rest assured, Masters. Old Xu has walked the roads of Lixia for over ten years. These traveling tricks are carved into my very bones. Had I ever traversed this Guli Road before, I could draw that map for you right this instant."

As Xu Wenshan hurried off in a whirlwind to find his crew, Li Tongya chewed his snake meat and spoke quietly to Li Yesheng, "Uncle has been bored idling at home, so it is better he comes out to manage some affairs. That Xu Wenshan is as cunning as a ghost. Even though we have planted mostly Liu family members in his ranks, you must still keep a watchful eye on him, Yesheng. Do not let Xu Wenshan gain too much power."

"I understand!"

Li Yesheng answered hastily. Having followed behind the two brothers for so long, he had gradually grown accustomed to managing affairs. In his heart, he began to secretly guard against Xu Wenshan.

"Tomorrow, find some reliable men of our own. Go up the mountain and build a courtyard around that banyan tree to prevent prying eyes..." After speaking, Li Xiangping waved his hand, and Li Yesheng tactfully withdrew.

Li Tongya then let out a soft sigh and said solemnly, "If not for the fact that our Li family truly lacks the manpower to hold down the fort, we would not need to be so endlessly calculating and guarded."

Li Xiangping kept his head down, eating his meat. He silently tightened his grip on his skewer and replied, "We should have visited the market. Buying a map would be the easiest thing in the world. However, our cultivation is meager, and the depths of that market are unknown. I fear we cannot afford the risk."

"Set your heart at ease!"

Seeing Li Xiangping's gloomy demeanor, Li Tongya laughed aloud and said.

"Now that Chejing has entered a sect, the spirit seedlings at home are growing well. The clan has produced a disciple with a spiritual aperture, and we have even discovered this spirit root. Our Li family is flourishing. If we are too greedy, it might instead invite sudden disaster."

Li Xiangping was momentarily stunned by these words. He nodded thoughtfully and smiled.

"I shall bear my elder brother's teachings in mind!"

---

Li Changfu had now moved near the main family estate, and his life had completely transformed. Not only had he reclaimed his ancestral surname, but whenever anyone in Lijing spoke of him, they would sigh and praise his incredible fortune!

Walking through the village, he might not be a tyrant, but he was certainly someone of standing. Who wouldn't respectfully greet Li Changfu when they saw him?

The elders of the Ye family were naturally green with envy. Even Liu Linfeng, who usually would not spare him a single glance, now had to smile and address him as Changfu.

To be fair, however, as proud as Li Changfu was, he still did not dare put on airs in front of Liu Linfeng. The man was, after all, the blood-uncle of the direct lineage. He might be down on his luck now, but who could say if he wouldn't stage a comeback one day?

And true enough, Liu Linfeng had only been down on his luck for a few months when a single command from the main family suddenly put him in charge of dozens of men. He was running from one end of the village to the other, drawing some cursed map. The smile on Liu Linfeng's face practically dripped onto the dirt!

Sitting in the small village tavern, Li Changfu savored his wine with great relish. Watching Liu Linfeng spin about day after day like a whipped top, he let out a dark chuckle.

_It seems your task isn't so easy either, Liu Linfeng. You certainly don't live as comfortably as I do!_

"Brother Changfu, Brother Changfu, tell us, what kind of miraculous methods do these immortals possess! Why do you lot keep running up the mountain day in and day out?"

Glancing at the idle men crowded around him, their eyes wide with eagerness for gossip, Li Changfu gave a cold snort.

He flicked his sleeves and barked, "Shut your mouths! Out of the way, all of you!"

He wasn't a fool. The Li family's numbers were thin, and as a commonborn son of the Li lineage, he was still considered a trusted confidant. He and his two children had been helping construct that very courtyard on the mountain. Li Yesheng had ordered strict secrecy. Would Li Changfu be stupid enough to sell out his own family just to entertain a crowd?

The middle-aged man had calculated it all perfectly in his heart. His own child possessed a spiritual aperture and carried the Li bloodline. Excluding the three currently in charge—Li Tongya, Li Xiangping, and Li Yesheng—that fourth seat of power was securely his! That Liu Rouxuan was an outsider; how could she ever meddle in their affairs?

Now that he and his three children—one an immortal seedling, the other two mortals—had all changed their surname back to Li, the Li family's interests were Li Qiuyang's interests, which in turn were his own interests. How could he ever sell out his own house?

"I'm off to work!"

Li Changfu waved his hand, downed the last of his clear wine, and swaggered his way out of the crowd of loafers.

The courtyard on the mountain was large, and the deadline was tight. Li Changfu had no time to waste loitering here.
