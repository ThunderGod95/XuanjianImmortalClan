# Chapter 37: Spirit Rice

Li Tongya felt his heart skip a beat, his vision spinning slightly. He hurriedly turned his head away, casting his gaze low toward the pale green spirit rice swaying unsteadily in the fields.

He said softly, "You... better wait until you have condensed the Profound Scenery Wheel before discussing anything else."

Liu Rouxuan giggled. Leaning close to Li Tongya's face, her beautiful brows relaxing, she whispered into his ear, "In that case, Brother Tongya must not run off with someone else first..."

Li Tongya instantly turned flush with embarrassment. Rising to his feet in somewhat bashful indignation, he said, "You should just focus on your cultivation!"

With that, he fled the courtyard as if escaping, vanishing into the distance.

---

Strolling along the stone path for quite some distance, Li Tongya splashed his face with river water and gradually regained his composure. 

He couldn't help but smile bitterly. "I am afraid she has me completely captivated."

"So Second Brother really did fall for Liu Rouxuan! Father is truly always one move ahead!"

Unexpectedly, Li Xiangping was sitting on the riverbank with a grin, laughing at Li Tongya. "Back when Father arranged for you to take Liu Rouxuan along, Second Brother, you only saw the external strategy: managing her away from her family to prevent their faction from growing too powerful. Yet, as a spectator who is blind to his own affairs, you failed to see the internal strategy directed entirely at you, Li Tongya."

"Third Brother, cease your teasing!" Li Tongya gave a bitter smile and shook his head helplessly, replying, "The more I observe Father, the more I feel there is always something to learn from the old man."

"Indeed!" Li Xiangping nodded, his expression turning serious. "As for the three Serpent Essence Pills Xiao Yuansi left behind, I intend for us to use them to break through the bottlenecks when we advance to the Jade Capital Wheel."

"Father and Yesheng have already dripped their blood on the Fog Bewildering Array. Later, I will secretly take the dharma mirror up to the mountain courtyard. As for the ⟨Profound Water Sword Art⟩... I fear I possess no talent for it. Second Brother, you should study it more."

Li Tongya scooped a handful of river water to wash his hands, replying, "Chejing has been at the immortal sect for nearly three years now, and the first batch of planted spirit rice is also about to mature. In the coming days, you and I will have no leisure time."

---

The snow blanketing Lijing was much heavier this year. In the fields, the spirit rice had finally reached maturity. Pale green husks cradled grains of spirit rice that glistened like white jade. They stood unyielding in the earth, their stalks and leaves remaining perfectly straight, refusing to bow even beneath the weight of the heavy snow.

This spirit rice was nothing like ordinary rice. Its leaves were sharp as blades, and the stalks typically required an axe to harvest. Li Tongya and the others had to coat their sickles with the ⟨Golden Light Art⟩ to finally finish reaping these several spirit fields.

Looking at the spirit rice piled into small green bundles, Li Qiuyang stepped on the snow, clapped his hands, and smiled. "Brother Xiangping, this spirit rice looks quite splendid."

Li Qiuyang had condensed the Profound Scenery Wheel last month, subsequently swearing the Profound Scenery Spirit Oath in the ancestral hall and learning a few dharma techniques. The child was now ten years old. With his facial features filling out, he appeared rather handsome and began to take on the demeanor of an adult.

He had originally accumulated the full eighty-one wisps of spiritual energy a year ago, but unfortunately fell short of success at the final hurdle when condensing the Profound Scenery Wheel. He had no choice but to spend another year breathing and regulating his qi to recover his spiritual energy. As a result, Liu Rouxuan was now rapidly catching up to him.

"Indeed." Li Xiangping was also brimming with smiles. His dharma power circulated endlessly within him; he had already successfully cultivated the Heavenly Circuit Wheel.

Tian Yun had delivered fraternal twins—a boy and a girl—bringing new life to the Li family and leaving Li Xiangping grinning from ear to ear. The Old Master looked at the children over and over again. Following the clan's generational naming characters, the boy was named Li Xuanfeng, and the girl was named Li Jingtian.

"The White Essence Fruit is not yet mature, but this spirit rice has finished being harvested. Let us bring it back first."

The village guards behind him had long since used shears to snip off the leaves of the spirit rice one by one before tying them with thick ropes. Only then did they dare reach out in pairs to move them. The leaves of the spirit rice were as sharp as knives, and the men had suffered quite a bit of hardship because of it.

Upon arriving at the foot of Mount Lijing, a thin mist gradually began to spread.

Li Yesheng said in a deep voice to the men behind him, "Stay close! If you get lost in this array formation, your puny lives will be forfeit!"

The village guards quickly voiced their obedience. Treading the stone path up to the summit, they placed the bundles of spirit rice in the courtyard.

Li Xiangping then waved his hand and said, "Take them back down."

To detach the spirit rice from the stalks required dharma power. Special dharma techniques had to be woven to husk the rice and remove the bran, ensuring the spiritual energy within the spirit rice did not dissipate.

The only thing mortals could do during the harvest of spirit rice was to cut it with sickles coated with the ⟨Golden Light Art⟩ and transport it. The truly crucial steps still had to be handled by immortal cultivators.

"Third Uncle! Clan Brother!" Li Xuanxuan walked out of the courtyard, cupping his hands toward Li Qiuyang and Li Xiangping. He was over seven years old now, his bodily foundation gradually filling out. His face bore a striking resemblance to the late Li Changhu.

"Xuanxuan, come here." Seeing Li Xuanxuan walking toward Li Qiuyang, Li Xiangping's expression shifted, and he hastily called him over.

Once Li Xuanxuan was seated beside him, Li Xiangping laughed and said, "This child had his spiritual aperture tested last year. His cultivation has been progressing at an astonishing pace. I reckon in a few more months, he will be able to condense the Profound Scenery Wheel."

Hearing this, Li Xuanxuan was taken aback. A multitude of doubts arose in his heart as he pondered inwardly, _The family clearly hasn't measured my root bone, nor have they passed down any dharma techniques to me. These words from Third Uncle..._

Outwardly, however, he maintained a cheerful smile, nodding at the envious Li Qiuyang.

"Xuan'er is truly amazing!" Li Qiuyang couldn't help but praise. Thinking of how it took him two full years to condense his Profound Scenery Wheel, he felt somewhat ashamed.

The two chatted idly for a while. Once Li Tongya came up the mountain, Li Xuanxuan withdrew. The group then spent several hours plucking the spirit rice and husking it.

Li Mutian divided the rice into sacks, lifted one gently, and said, "One hundred and twenty catties of spirit rice, and forty catties of spirit rice bran."

"In two years, we will be able to harvest another crop. This should be enough to pay the tributes."

---

After Li Xiangping saw Li Qiuyang off, he placed the two sacks of spirit rice and bran on the table to examine them. Seeing every grain of spirit rice looking as pristine and beautiful as white jade, he couldn't help but sigh in admiration.

Only then did he place the rice inside the room and say softly to Li Tongya, "Xuanxuan is seven years old. His bodily foundation is set. It is time for him to cultivate."

Li Tongya was momentarily stunned. He nodded thoughtfully and followed Li Xiangping into the room.

A charcoal fire burned in the room. The elderly were prone to lethargy in winter, so Li Mutian had gone to sleep early, leaving only Li Xuanxuan in the hall, leaning against the stove to warm himself.

Inside the room, Li Xuanxuan watched the snowflakes drifting down over the mountain, staring blankly at the silver-clad Mount Lijing.

Seeing the two men enter, he quickly stood up and asked, "Third Uncle, why did you tell Clan Brother that I am close to condensing the Profound Scenery Wheel? If I do not have a spiritual aperture, wouldn't that be a massive embarrassment..."

Li Xiangping carefully closed the doors and windows. Giving Li Xuanxuan a shushing gesture, he said in a deep voice, "Follow me."

Li Xuanxuan immediately fell silent. He obediently trailed behind Li Xiangping, walking to a grey-brick, tiled-roof room nestled deepest in the courtyard.

Li Xiangping took out a key, unlocked the door, and gently pushed it open.

Looking at Li Xuanxuan, he whispered, "Come in."

Seeing Li Xiangping's grave expression, Li Xuanxuan also grew anxious and uneasy. He noticed the room was quite spacious, containing nothing but a bluestone pedestal perfectly in the center. Mounted upon the pedestal was a greyish-blue dharma mirror.

Incense burned inside the room, its fragrance soothing and calming to the spirit. Li Xuanxuan cast a low glance at Li Xiangping. Seeing his uncle staring silently at the mirror, the boy didn't dare to speak.
