# Chapter 20: Acknowledging a Master

Seeing that azure-clad man, who resembled an immortal, slowly float down into the courtyard, a bitter taste immediately filled Li Xiangping's mouth as he cursed inwardly, _Motherfucker, I fear the owner of that mirror has come looking for it!_

He was instantly seized by terror and unease, feeling only that this person was quite divine and unfathomably deep. He looked up at Li Chejing and Li Tongya; although the two maintained composed expressions, panic and unease lurked in the depths of their eyes, clearly sharing the exact same thought as Li Xiangping.

"I am the master of Qingsui Peak of the Azure Pond Sect."

Si Yuanbai smiled faintly. Seeing the extreme vigilance of the Li family members, rather than bowing in worship like ordinary mortals, further solidified his judgment.

He answered with a smile, "You gentlemen need not be tense. This Guli Road is under the jurisdiction of my Azure Pond Sect. Since you have embarked on the immortal path, you naturally fall under the governance of the Azure Pond Sect."

Realizing this person was not here for the mirror, Li Tongya and the others immediately relaxed, their hostility toward Si Yuanbai significantly lessening.

Li Tongya cupped his fists and spoke respectfully, "We are but lowly villagers, ignorant and ill-informed. We stepped into the Profound Scenery purely by accident and and were ignorant of your esteemed sect's grand prestige. We have indeed slighted the exalted envoy."

Si Yuanbai paused, instantly understanding the situation. Guli Road had not seen anyone sent to manage it for hundreds of years, so these villagers did not even know the name of the Azure Pond Sect. Thus, he formed an incantation, and a lifelike map of the Yue State materialized before the crowd.

This display immediately shocked the Li family. Although Li Chejing and the others had learned incantations and could summon dharma power, they struggled to even mold it into a basic shape, let alone exercise the exquisite control required to construct a map.

"This is the Yue State."

With a shift of Si Yuanbai's thoughts, an azure patch suddenly appeared on the southern portion of the Yue State in his hands. This patch occupied roughly a quarter of the entire Yue State.

"This is my Azure Pond Sect."

"So it is the Azure Pond Immortal Sect," Li Mutian cupped his hands and said, "When I was young, I followed General Yang to attack Mount Yue. Those talismans must have been distributed by the Azure Pond Immortal Sect."

"We absolutely cannot bear the title of an immortal sect," Si Yuanbai waved his hands repeatedly.

He then addressed Li Xiangping and his brothers, "Since you have entered the gates of the Profound Scenery, the several villages along Guli Road shall be placed under your governance. You need only remember to offer the cultivation resources demanded by the sect year after year."

"There are still several other clans along Guli Road. Your family's foundation is shallow; in all matters, yield where you can, and do not engage in excessive disputes. Every five years, the sect will send someone to take people up the mountain. If an outstanding individual were to emerge from your clan, it would be of immense benefit to your family."

Li Tongya's expression changed slightly as he inquired in a low voice, "May I ask about these resources..."

"Once every five years, each offering consisting of ten White Essence Fruits and two hundred catties[^1] of spirit rice."

Si Yuanbai spoke with a stern expression, "These resources absolutely cannot fall short. It would be best if you had a disciple enter the sect's mountain gate[^2]; otherwise, severe deductions and exploitation are inevitable..."

With Si Yuanbai making it this explicit, how could Li Tongya not understand his meaning? The brothers exchanged glances, and Li Mutian thought inwardly, _This man came neither early nor late, appearing exactly when Jing'er broke through. He most likely wants Jing'er to join that Azure Pond Sect._

_That is fine, too. Jing'er's innate talent is outstanding; it is truly an injustice to keep him cooped up deep in these mountains. It is better for him to go to the immortal sect to spread his wings, which will also provide backing for the family both inside and out._

Thinking of this, Li Mutian gave Li Chejing a slight nod. Seeing his father's expression, Li Chejing immediately understood.

He bowed deeply to Si Yuanbai and spoke with utmost reverence, "Chejing is dull in talent, yet I admire the Peak Master's elegance. I hope to acknowledge you as my master and serve as a sweeping boy under your tutelage, respectfully listening to your immortal teachings."

"Excellent. It is fitting that you enter my Qingsui Peak."

Si Yuanbai did not stand on ceremony. Nodding with a smile, he patted his waist with his left hand, and two bundles—one large and one small—appeared on the ground.

Looking at their amazed expressions, he explained, "This is a storage bag. Once you have cultivated to the Jade Capital wheel, you will be able to use one."

"The larger of these two bundles contains the seeds for the spirit rice, and the smaller holds the pits of the White Essence Fruit."

Flipping his left hand, Si Yuanbai revealed a few wooden slips and continued, "There is no need for you to be a sweeping boy. Since you have acknowledged me as your master, you should know my name. My surname is Si and my given name is Yuanbai. I am a Peak Master in the Azure Pond Sect, so naturally, I will not treat you unjustly."

"These three scrolls contain arts used by rogue cultivators of the Yue State, which your family can study for reference. The other scroll details the cultivation methods for the spirit rice and the White Essence Fruit. You must study it diligently!"

The men assented in low voices.

Si Yuanbai smiled and asked, "There is no need to be so nervous. What arts are you cultivating?"

Li Xiangping and the others instantly tensed as if facing a mortal enemy. They exchanged glances, and for a moment, no one spoke.

Seeing them stammering and hesitating, Si Yuanbai suddenly understood. 

He waved his hands repeatedly, "I understand, I understand. I presume that senior gave you instructions."

Si Yuanbai had only successfully reached Foundation Establishment last year, so he naturally considered the Foundation Establishment cultivator from ten years ago to be his senior.

Hearing this, Li Mutian coughed and stepped forward from behind them, speaking in a low voice, "Over ten years ago, this old man saved a senior. That senior recovered from his injuries in my home for several years, left behind this cultivation art, and instructed this old man never to reveal it. After speaking, he drifted away..."

"Indeed!"

Si Yuanbai nodded lightly, inwardly quite proud of his own astuteness. 

He pondered, _It is nothing more than a wheel-nurturing method anyway. Could it possibly be some immortal art that harvests the essence of sun and moon? There is no need to force the issue and risk hurting my disciple's feelings._

Gazing at the old man with pity, Si Yuanbai flipped his palm to reveal a pale azure jade seal. With a gentle stroke across it, the seal slowly lit up with golden light, and several large golden characters materialized—**Under Azure Pond's Rule**.

Si Yuanbai looked at the crowd and said, "This is the token of vassalage to my Azure Pond Sect. I have already imprinted a dharma seal upon it. With this, you can demarcate your territory from neighboring clans, and you can also use it to seek aid from the sect. Keep it safe."

Supporting the air with his left hand, the jade seal slowly descended into Li Tongya's hands.

Watching Li Tongya stow the jade seal away safely, Si Yuanbai smiled and turned his head to say to Li Chejing, "My good disciple, bid your family farewell."

With red eyes, Li Chejing hugged his two older brothers. He kowtowed several times before Li Mutian and said tearfully, "Your son is unfilial. From now on, I will not be able to serve by your knees. I can only hope that you will take great care of your health."

Li Mutian coughed a few times and hurriedly helped Li Chejing up, instructing, "You need not worry about matters at home; your two brothers are here. Cultivate diligently within the sect and become a pillar of support for our Li family."

After bidding farewell to his two brothers as well, Li Chejing reluctantly walked to Si Yuanbai's side and replied, "Master, your disciple has bidden farewell to his family."

Si Yuanbai nodded repeatedly, paying no mind to Li Tongya and the others bowing deeply. Instead, he laughed heartily and muttered to himself, "Having accepted a fine disciple today, I shall let that pig demon off the hook!"

With that, he took hold of Li Chejing on the ground and waved his hand gently. A flying shuttle slowly materialized before him. Forming another incantation with his hands, the flying shuttle forcibly stretched out several times its original length. The two stepped onto the flying shuttle and shot off toward the north like a shooting star.

[^1]: 1 Catty = 500g or 1.1023lb
[^2]: In ancient China, prominent Daoist and Buddhist temples were typically built on remote, spiritually rich mountains. The grand archway at the base of the trail leading up to the temple was literally the "mountain gate."

    Metaphorically, to "enter the mountain gate" (拜山门 - bài shānmén, literally "to bow at the mountain gate") means to officially become an inner or recognized disciple of the sect.
