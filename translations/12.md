# Chapter 12: Golden Light Art

For the sixth time, Li Changhu jolted awake from his meditation. Gritting his teeth, he sat cross-legged once more to calm his state of mind.

_Too difficult._

After cultivating for over three months, he had only condensed a mere handful of wisps of lunar essence. Xiangping was already attempting to condense the Profound Scenery Wheel, and Tongya had formed over seventy wisps of lunar essence. Yet he, Li Changhu, was still spinning his wheels in the single digits.

Compared to his two younger brothers' effortless and graceful cultivation, Li Changhu found guiding spiritual energy across the Twelve-Story Pagoda to be incredibly strenuous. Even the talisman seed in his Qihai aperture was listless and dim.

When refining lunar essence, let alone during the day, he could not even perceive the lunar essence on nights when the moonlight was slightly poor. These few wisps of lunar essence he currently possessed were solely condensed by relying on the dharma mirror.

_Sigh._

Li Changhu already knew that he had absolutely no talent on the path of immortal cultivation. Whenever it was his turn to cultivate using the dharma mirror, he felt an overwhelming sense of guilt, feeling as though he was blocking his younger brothers' paths to the Dao.

Jolting awake from his meditation for the seventh time, Li Changhu's expression was grim. He looked at Li Tongya and Li Xiangping beside him, quietly stood up, and walked out the door.

In the rear courtyard, Li Mutian was bent over, sharpening a long blade.

Gripping the back of the blade with his right hand and pinching the body of the blade with the thumb and index finger of his left, he held the weapon steadily, grinding it back and forth against the whetstone.

Li Changhu hardened his expression and spoke, "Father."

"What is it?" Li Mutian did not even look up, his voice indifferent.

"I fear I am not cut out for immortal cultivation," Li Changhu replied, somewhat ashamed.

"Then you will simply stop cultivating?" Li Mutian raised his head, staring at him coldly.

"It is not that I will stop cultivating." Li Changhu had already prepared his response and explained as he looked at his father, "My natural talent falls far short of Tongya and Xiangping, to say nothing of Jing'er. I can only barely cultivate by relying on the dharma mirror. During the day, it would be better for me to help you manage the family estate and patrol the fields, so as not to arouse anyone's suspicions."

"So be it."

Li Mutian pondered for a moment. His expression softened significantly as he shook his head and said, "You can stop tomorrow. Go and prepare for Xiangping's wedding. Do not make it too extravagant; just invite some relatives and keep it low-profile."

"Yes." Li Changhu nodded heavily and hurried toward the main courtyard.

Yet, upon entering the main courtyard, he saw his wife, Lady Ren, sitting there. She was sewing clothes while chatting with Tian Yun. The female family members were not permitted to enter the rear courtyard's ancestral hall, so they had no choice but to wait here.

Seeing Li Changhu emerge, Tian Yun figured the couple had matters to discuss, so she smiled, made an excuse, and returned to the Tian household.

"What is the matter, Ping'er?" Upon seeing his wife, Li Changhu's features softened considerably, and he asked her gently.

"Changhu."

Ren Ping'er possessed an elegant and dignified beauty. A hint of joy danced in her eyes as she whispered, "I am expecting."

"What?" Li Changhu was deeply shocked, all the oppressive frustration in his mind vanishing without a trace. His face flushed slightly as he asked back excitedly, "Are you really expecting?"

"As if I would lie to you. Mother-in-law has already checked; I really am." Ren Ping'er nodded bashfully, lightly chiding him.

"Father, Father! Ping'er is expecting!"

Li Changhu laughed heartily, overcome with joy, and immediately yelled toward the rear courtyard.

Clang...

With a sharp clatter of iron, Li Mutian instantly abandoned his blade and rushed to the main courtyard.

With an agitated expression, he asked, "What did you say, boy?"

"Ping'er is expecting!" Li Changhu grinned foolishly like a child.

"Good! Good."

Li Mutian laughed uproariously. He patted the blushing Ren Ping'er on the shoulder and declared loudly to Li Changhu, "I am going to fetch the physician. Watch over her carefully."

Li Changhu nodded repeatedly, led Ren Ping’er to a seat, and began carefully asking after her health and comfort.

---

The rear courtyard.

Li Xiangping formed a hand seal and slowly awoke from his meditation.

Reaching out, he picked up the wooden slip bundle standing before him, untied the cloth binding, and unrolled it with a clatter.

Beneath the bright moonlight, the large characters at the head of the scroll were starkly visible:

⟨Golden Light Art⟩

While the ⟨Supreme Yin Breathing Wheel-Nurturing Scripture⟩ could neither be spoken aloud nor written down, its accompanying incantations could be orally taught and transcribed. Li Tongya and the others had thus copied down the arts they received to share with one another.

The scroll of the ⟨Golden Light Art⟩ before him was the art Li Chejing had acquired. Li Xiangping had successfully formed his Profound Scenery Wheel a few nights prior, so he had borrowed this art to practice.

The ⟨Golden Light Art⟩ was not difficult. After reading and cross-referencing it for two nights, Li Xiangping had fully comprehended its crux.

Flipping his left hand, a radiant and colorful golden gleam emerged from his palm.

"⟨Golden Light Art⟩!"

This was an art of offense and defense. By forming the seal and casting the art, one could condense a golden gleam of exceptional sharpness.

This golden gleam could be attached to a blade or sword to strike enemies, or it could be hurled outward as a projectile, carrying considerable might.

Waving his hand to dispel the art, Li Xiangping stepped out of the courtyard gates. His older brothers were all cultivating cross-legged. 

He found a piece of bluestone near the entrance, wrapped both arms around it, and shouted softly, "Up!"

Unexpectedly, Li Xiangping lifted the bluestone with great ease. Because he had exerted too much force, he even stumbled heavily backward a few steps with his head tilted back.

"Stepping into Fetal Breathing realm grants a lifespan of one hundred and twenty years. With dharma power flowing through apertures and meridians, the body becomes light, strength surges, hearing becomes sharp, and vision clears. Casting arts through hand seals separates one from the mortal masses."

Li Xiangping recalled the descriptions within the ⟨Supreme Yin Breathing Wheel-Nurturing Scripture⟩ and could not help but chuckle. Carrying the bluestone, he returned to the rear courtyard.

He set the bluestone down properly, retreated ten metres, and cast the art by forming the seal. A golden light leaped from his palm, slashing straight onto the bluestone.

Clang~

Bright sparks flashed as a moderately deep blade mark appeared on the bluestone's surface. The stone itself was pushed back a a hand's breadth[^1] distance, leaving faint white friction scratches on the ground.

"What a fine art!"

It was Li Tongya, who had already awoken from his meditation, looking at Li Xiangping with a smile, "Jing'er's art is the most useful to possess. A few days ago, he cast it and accidentally sliced off a piece of the artificial rockery in the rear courtyard. I didn't even dare tell Father."

"When it comes to cultivation, we truly cannot compare to Jing'er." Li Xiangping curled his lip, straightened the bluestone, and estimated the remaining dharma power in his body. He could probably cast the art another four to five times.

"Jing'er entered the Profound Scenery stage in less than a month. Even with the dharma mirror's assistance, his speed is far too astonishing." Li Tongya shook his head.

The Six Wheels of Fetal Breathing were respectively named Profound Scenery, Bearing Brightness, Heavenly Circuit, Azure Essence, Jade Capital, and Nascent Spirit. Among them, the Profound Scenery, Heavenly Circuit, and Jade Capital wheels were known as the Three Hurdles of Fetal Breathing, and were exceptionally difficult to overcome.

Li Chejing and the others had easily breached the gateway to Fetal Breathing with the help of the dharma mirror. But for the subsequent Bearing Brightness Wheel, there were no shortcuts left to take; they would simply have to dutifully breathe and absorb spiritual energy to condense their fetal breath.

"Guiding qi to refine essence to reach Profound Scenery in nine months, and breathing and absorbing to reach Bearing Brightness in three months is considered possessing superior aptitude and bone structure."

Li Xiangping sat beside his second brother, reciting the descriptions from the wheel-nurturing scripture, and said to Li Tongya, "Since we relied on an external object, the standard for Profound Scenery cannot be counted. But this three-month standard for Bearing Brightness is something we can certainly use to measure ourselves."

"Indeed," Li Tongya nodded slightly, then lowered his voice. "Eldest Brother, he..."

"I am afraid Eldest Brother is even worse off than you and me," Li Xiangping replied somewhat gloomily, gazing quietly at the Great Li Mountains beneath the night sky.

"I will regulate my breath for a few more days, and then I can attempt to condense the Profound Scenery Wheel. If I succeed, the dharma mirror can be entirely dedicated to Eldest Brother's cultivation," Li Tongya pondered.

Li Xiangping, who was gazing at the Great Li Mountains, suddenly narrowed his eyes. He stood up abruptly, stared toward the rear mountain, and shouted sternly, "What gall, you thief!"

[^1]: In the metric system, this is roughly 7.5 to 10 cm.
