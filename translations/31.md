# Chapter 31: Wan Tiancang

Wan Xiaohua sat before his desk, unable to conceal the exhaustion etched across his face.

A cultivator of the Jade Capital Wheel had already condensed a spiritual wheel within the Ascendant Sun Palace; logically speaking, he should remain vigorous even after three days and nights without sleep.

Yet, with too many vexing matters piling up recently, even Wan Xiaohua felt himself buckling under the strain.

"The letter sent over by the messenger from the Li family—what are your thoughts on it, Yuankai?" Massaging his temples, Wan Xiaohua looked wearily at the crowd seated below him.

Before Wan Yuankai could even open his mouth, a burly man beside him blurted out brashly, "What is there to think? This Li family is weak in strength but rich in delusions. With only two men holding up their family, Patriarch, you could storm right to their door single-handedly and force them to surrender all those spirit fields to our Wan family!"

The man had thick brows and large eyes, his tiger-like gaze staring valiantly at Wan Xiaohua above.

_Fool!_ Wan Xiaohua cursed inwardly.

Aloud, he countered, "The Li family has someone cultivating within the sect. If they send an inquiry demanding answers, how shall we account for ourselves?"

"This..."

Seeing the burly man temporarily rendered speechless, Wan Yuankai finally stepped forward and said, "If the Li family demands three-tenths, then we shall give them three-tenths. The clan's stockpile is dwindling by the year, and it looks as though we will soon be unable to pay our tributes. The survival of our clan must take precedence above all else."

Wan Xiaohua nodded, adding softly, "The three-tenths can likely still be negotiated. The Ji family wields immense power. Understanding the peril of lips perishing and teeth freezing, the Li family will not push us too far."

At the mention of the Ji family, Wan Xiaohua's expression turned sinister. Gently setting down the letter in his hand, he continued, "Just a few years ago, Yubu Mountain served as our border. Now, we have been pushed all the way back to Guli Road. That old thief Ji Dengqi pushes others too far. Our strength has been lacking, and over these years of repeated concessions, we have not been afforded a single chance to catch our breath."

Wan Yuankai gnashed his teeth and nodded, letting out a long sigh. "Fortunately, we have found a way out of an impasse. The gradual restoration of the spiritual mechanisms and the earth vein at the northern foothills of the Great Li Mountains has provided our Wan family with a glimmer of hope. It is just a pity that it is not in our hands."

Yet Wan Xiaohua let out a light chuckle, his voice cold. "The current situation is already for the best. Even if our Wan family were to occupy the northern foothills of the Great Li Mountains, we would require manpower to guard it. If I were to dispatch you lot, who would remain to defend our ancestral home here on Mount Huaqian? Furthermore, this matter is of grave importance. If it were to alert the Ji family, would Ji Dengqi just stand idly by and watch our Wan family expand?"

"In secret, Ji Dengqi has been sending men to contact the foolish branch families under our rule, enticing the masses to play both sides. Contacting the Li family or sending men over must be done with the utmost secrecy. Under no circumstances can Ji Dengqi be allowed to catch wind of this!"

Wan Yuankai nodded heavily, replying, "I have already arranged for my younger cousin to fake his death and slip away to Lidaokou. Ji Dengqi's eyes and ears are nothing but mere mortals; they will notice nothing."

"Tiancang is eloquent in speech, calm, and prudent. I am most at ease entruting this matter to him." With that, Wan Xiaohua lifted a brush and traced a light hook on the map before him. His gaze grew cold as he murmured, "A blunt knife slicing flesh... yet he should also beware of our Wan family biting back."

---

Li Tongya regulated his breathing, sensing the ceaseless surging of dharma power within his body. He let out a light chuckle and sighed with emotion, "Circulating endlessly without rest... After nearly four years, it is finally accomplished."

"Congratulations, Brother Ya, on taking a step further and successfully cultivating the Heavenly Circuit Wheel!" Beside him, Liu Rouxuan smiled brightly, tucking a loose strand of hair behind her ear. Her dark eyes gazed directly at Li Tongya as she offered her joyful congratulations.

"I am merely a minor cultivator at the Fetal Breathing realm. It is nothing worth congratulating!" Li Tongya gently turned his head away, discreetly evading Liu Rouxuan's gaze as he replied with a slight trace of fluster.

"Hehe."

Watching Liu Rouxuan giggle foolishly to herself over something, Li Tongya shook his head in resignation.

Checking the color of the sky, he said, "I have been in secluded cultivation for a day and a night. I must go see Xiangping first."

"Understood," Liu Rouxuan nodded vigorously with a smile.

Li Tongya left the small Lijing courtyard and had just reached the Li family's front yard when he faintly heard noises coming from the main courtyard, causing him to halt his steps.

"Mother! Why does everyone else have a father, but I am the only one who does not!"

Lady Ren was sitting quietly in the courtyard, sewing garments, when Li Xuanxuan fell to his knees.

Gripping Ren Ping'er's dress, his voice thick with tears, he spoke with utter grievance, "Even Li Xiewen has a father! Where did my father go?"

Hearing this, Lady Ren hurriedly squatted down to embrace Li Xuanxuan. Choking back a sob, she answered, "Your father went to a place very, very far away..."

"Mother is lying to me! Mother is lying!" Li Xuanxuan forcefully broke free from Ren Ping'er's embrace, crying out, "Who travels far away and never sends a letter home! They all say Daddy is dead!"

Ren Ping'er was instantly struck dumb, gazing helplessly at the weeping and wailing Li Xuanxuan.

Listening from afar, Li Tongya felt a pang of sorrow in his heart. Letting out a long sigh, he strode into the main courtyard and gently scooped Li Xuanxuan into his arms.

Seeing Li Tongya, Li Xuanxuan immediately wiped his tears and sobbed, "Second Uncle, where is my father!"

Li Tongya gently patted Li Xuanxuan's back, a bitter sorrow in his voice as he replied, "Your father was a good man. He was a generous and kind older brother..."

"Wuwuwu..."

Just as he was consoling Li Xuanxuan, Li Yesheng knocked and entered the front yard. Upon seeing him, Lady Ren hurriedly pulled the weeping Li Xuanxuan away, leaving only the two men in the courtyard.

"What is the matter?" Li Tongya reined in his emotions and asked in a deep voice.

"There is a man dressed as a hunter requesting an audience at the gate. He claims to be the one sent by the Wan family to fulfill the agreement."

Li Tongya was momentarily taken aback. Stroking his chin, he waved a hand at Li Yesheng, "Let him in."

Li Yesheng then led the man inside. He had an ordinary appearance, wore leather armor, and carried a longbow on his back. However, his eyes were remarkably bright. Stepping forward, he looked at Li Tongya and cupped his hands in greeting.

"Wan Tiancang of the Wan family, paying his respects to the Young Patriarch!"

Li Tongya raised a brow.

_This man far surpasses Wan Yuankai. I fear he will not be easy to deal with,_ he thought inwardly.

Smiling, he replied, "I cannot bear the title of Young Patriarch. I merely manage the trivial affairs of the village."

With a wave of his hand, he added, "Please, come in."

A glint of surprise flashed through Wan Tiancang's eyes.

_I heard the Old Master of the Li family rarely shows his face, leaving the two brothers to jointly rule the Li family without any distinction between primary and secondary,_ he mused silently. _From the looks of it today, this Li Tongya speaks with careful prudence. I wonder what sort of character that Li Xiangping might be._

Though his mind raced with speculations, he smiled outwardly and said, "You are too polite."

Wan Tiancang entered the main courtyard. The old man was seated at the head of the room, sipping tea. Casting his eyes downward, he offered Wan Tiancang nothing more than a faint nod.

Observing Li Mutian's extraordinary bearing and unparalleled composure, Wan Tiancang marveled inwardly, _This man must be the Old Master of the Li family. He truly lives up to his reputation as a senior who once reached Foundation Establishment. His demeanor is quite unlike that of an ordinary man._

He hurriedly knelt in a deep bow. By the time he rose, his face was already streaked with tears.

Choking on his words, he said, "Paying respects to Senior."

Li Mutian set down his teabowl and said in a deep voice, "What has brought you to such a state?"

Wan Tiancang wiped his tears and replied, "Seeing Senior's majestic demeanor reminded this junior of my own father. The old man suffered at the poisonous hands of Ji Dengqi! Following that, our clan lost nine hundred catties of spirit rice. We are on the verge of being unable to pay our tributes for the year after next!"

"Nine hundred catties?" Li Tongya exchanged a glance with Li Mutian seated above, a look of mutual comprehension flashing through both their eyes.

_This man wants to touch our three-tenths share of the harvest!_ Li Tongya thought to himself.
