# Chapter 26: Serpent

Dark brown patterns crisscrossed the serpent's body, which was as thick as the waist of an eight-year-old child. Coiled tightly around a massive banyan tree, it stared coldly at Li Tongya and the others with bright yellow, slitted pupils.

"What a massive serpent!" Though marveling verbally, Li Tongya's hands were not slow in forming hand seals. The moment his words fell, a hazy golden radiance had already surfaced in his hands.

The serpent hissed, flicking its bright red tongue. Opening its massive maw, it forcefully spewed out a thick cloud of black mist.

The golden light arrived first, striking straight at the dark-patterned serpent's jaw. The blow knocked the serpent's head askew, and the thick black mist instantly poured onto a large tree to the side.

In an instant, the large banyan tree sizzled. Its lush branches and leaves were completely corroded away in a flash, turning into drops of black liquid that fell upon the soil. Even the main trunk, thick enough for two men to embrace, had a thick layer eaten away.

A sharp gasp went around the group.

Seeing this, Li Yesheng and the others were so terrified they retreated repeatedly, looking as if they were a hair's breadth away from turning and running.

Having saved Li Yesheng and the village men from the serpent's clutches, Li Tongya shook his head gravely and spoke in a low voice, "Keep your eyes on it, and retreat slowly."

Watching the men slowly retreat, the serpent did not give chase. Coiled around the large banyan tree, it stared coldly at the group.

Only when the large banyan tree disappeared from view did Li Qiuyang speak timidly, "The fruit is right behind that tree!"

"There is no rush," Li Tongya waved his hand and instructed Li Yesheng, "Go gather the village men. Have every household bring their long staffs and iron shovels. Those with bows are even better; bring bows and arrows and come up the mountain together."

"Yes."

Li Yesheng acknowledged the order and hurried down the mountain with those few village men.

Li Xiangping patted Li Qiuyang's shoulder and said, "You must have arrived just when the serpent was out hunting for food back then. Not only did you escape with your life, but you also stumbled upon an opportunity! Return to the courtyard and cultivate. You are no longer needed here."

Seeing Li Qiuyang covered in cold sweat as he agreed and headed down the mountain, Li Xiangping found a bluestone to sit on and said to Li Tongya, "This serpent has incredibly tough scales. The ⟨Golden Light Art⟩ only managed to shatter a few scales on its jaw."

"Judging by its appearance, it is merely a minor demon at the Fetal Breathing realm. Its spiritual intelligence remains unawakened. No matter how tough a beast's scales are, what use is it?" Li Tongya replied indifferently, carefully observing the terrain.

Before long, the strong men of Lijing came up the mountain one after another. Hearing they were going to kill a demon, they were both apprehensive and excited, tiptoeing closely behind Li Yesheng.

Having surveyed the terrain, Li Tongya called over Liu Linfeng and asked, "Uncle, I heard the Liu clan used a trap to kill a wild boar last year. For a serpent this large, how large of a trap should we dig?"

Liu Linfeng hesitated for a moment, asking cautiously, "I heard this serpent is a demon, and cannot be judged by common sense. What if it can fly..."

"Naturally, it cannot fly. Simply take your past experience and double it," Li Xiangping spoke up, silently muttering to himself, _If this serpent could fly, how would we still have our lives right now?_

"Then it is easy!"

Hearing Li Xiangping's words, Liu Linfeng immediately gave a full guarantee. He directed the men to start digging the pit, pointing here and there with quite an imposing air.

Liu Linfeng had been suppressing himself for a long time. In the past, although the Li family was powerful, they still gave his Liu clan some face, consulting them when village matters arose.

Now that the Li family had consolidated the villages, how could Liu Linfeng dare to step forward and issue orders? He had been suffocating these past few days. Finally having something to do, Liu Linfeng instantly regained his vigor.

_This cannot go on. My Liu clan must find a place to prove our worth under the Li family's protection, otherwise we will inevitably decline sooner or later!_ Liu Linfeng thought secretly as he directed the work.

Li Tongya had no idea Liu Linfeng was going through so much internal drama. He and Li Xiangping were busy imbuing the arrows with the ⟨Golden Light Art⟩. If left unused, the spell could be maintained for about twelve to fourteen hours, which was enough to kill this serpent.

With their current dharma power, the two could each enchant about a dozen arrows. Once their dharma power was exhausted, they meditated to regulate their breathing.

By the time the crowd had dug the pit, concealed the traces of fresh soil, and laid down dead leaves, the two had just finished regulating their breathing.

The village men set up their formation. Those holding long staffs stood at the front, while those behind held bows, each gripping an arrow imbued with the ⟨Golden Light Art⟩. They carefully advanced toward the serpent.

As soon as the men at the front reached the large banyan tree, they could not help but exclaim in unison, "What a terrifying serpent!"

The serpent was coiled around the large banyan tree. Its bright yellow vertical pupils left the men trembling in fear. Opening its mouth, it spat out a thick cloud of black mist.

However, the few men in the front row had been warned beforehand. They had long since dropped their staffs and retreated. The black mist only managed to corrode a patch of dead leaves on the ground and several long staffs.

Streaks of faint golden light, launched later, arrived first. These arrows, imbued with the ⟨Golden Light Art⟩, were incomparably sharp. One by one, they hooked into the dark-patterned serpent's scales.

Having been disturbed time and time again, the serpent finally flew into a rage. Hissing and flicking its tongue, it abandoned the banyan tree and slithered toward the crowd.

The men at the front instantly scattered. Li Tongya and Li Xiangping hurriedly formed hand seals and unleashed streams of golden light, striking the serpent until it shrieked in pain. It raised its golden pupils, locked onto the two men, and rapidly lunged toward them.

Rather than being alarmed, the two were delighted.

Watching the serpent slither along the slope of the cliff, they shouted loudly, "Prepare!"

As the serpent moved forward, it felt the ground beneath it give way, nearly diving headfirst into the large hole hidden beneath the dead leaves.

However, the serpent's body was quite long. It did not immediately fall into the trap. Its head quickly raised and rested on the opposite edge of the pit, looking as if it were about to climb out.

At the sound, a group of village men suddenly emerged on the cliff above. Pushing a massive bluestone together, they shouted in unison, "Strike!"

The bluestone plummeted instantly, smashing squarely onto the serpent's back. In a flash, tendons snapped and bones fractured. The dark-patterned serpent gave a hiss as it was immediately pinned to the bottom of the pit. Its head reared high, its upper body bent into a U-shape, but it was unable to move.

"Hahahahaha!"

From behind the crowd, Liu Linfeng let out a long laugh. Seeing his masterpiece succeed, he nodded in satisfaction.

"Attack!" Li Yesheng shouted loudly, and the crowd picked up sharpened bamboo poles, thrusting them relentlessly into the pit.

Although the serpent's scales were uniformly tough, it could not withstand being tormented like this by over a dozen burly men surrounding it. The crowd stabbed forcefully for half an hour, only stopping when the bamboo poles they wielded were dyed completely red.

After repeatedly confirming the serpent was dead, the crowd's cheers shook the heavens. Each and every one of them was incredibly smug, talking over one another as they discussed how to haul the serpent out and carry it back to the village.

Leaving Liu Linfeng behind to manage affairs, Li Tongya and Li Xiangping took Li Yesheng and a few trusted aides, quietly circling around to the back of the large banyan tree.

Seeing the ground covered in white bones amidst the dense thicket of grass, Li Yesheng sighed and spoke, "When Li Yecheng went up the rear mountain a few years ago and never returned, he must have run into this serpent foraging for food and was devoured by it."

Li Xiangping nodded very naturally. Li Tongya, however, gazed at the small tree bearing five bright, translucent red fruits, his expression grave. 

Turning to Li Yesheng, he said, "Take your men and guard this place. Do not let anyone approach."
