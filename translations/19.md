# Chapter 19: Cultivator

That night, the moonlight was bright and pure.

Li Chejing sat cross-legged in the courtyard, focusing his qi and settling his mind, setting out to condense the third wheel, the Heavenly Circuit Wheel.

The human body holds boundless mysteries, containing twelve meridians and the upper, middle, and lower dantians.

The ⟨Supreme Yin Breathing Wheel-Nurturing Scripture⟩ mentions, "The lower dantian is the mansion where essence is stored; the middle dantian is the mansion where qi is stored; the upper dantian is the mansion where spirit is stored."

Among the Six Wheels of Fetal Breathing, the Profound Scenery, Heavenly Circuit, and Jade Capital wheels are known as the Three Hurdles of Fetal Breathing, and are particularly difficult to cultivate. The crux lies in the fact that these three wheels must be condensed out of thin air within different dantians to form spiritual wheels.

The remaining three wheels are relatively easier. Just as the Profound Scenery Wheel resides in the Qihai aperture of the lower dantian, once condensed, one only needs to diligently breathe and refine qi, and the Bearing Brightness Wheel will naturally be born from the Qihai aperture.

The Heavenly Circuit Wheel, however, is located in the Juque pavilion[^1], which is the exact mansion of the human body where qi is stored. Once condensed, dharma power will flow without cease, circulating through the entire body. If this dharma power is applied to the eyes, one can see for a thousand miles; if applied to the feet, one can walk with divine speed, making leaping over eaves and running on walls a trivial matter.

Li Chejing rested for a quarter of an hour. Feeling his Qihai aperture brim full, with the two spiritual wheels orbiting and intertwining endlessly with one another, he formed a hand incantation. He guided his qi to flow through the Shimen, Guanyuan, Shenque, and Mingmen apertures[^2], gathering it within the Juque pavilion in an attempt to condense the spiritual wheel.

Lu Jiangxian was currently urging his divine sense to watch Li Chejing, casting a mind-calming incantation upon him while acting as his dharma protector alongside Li Tongya and Li Xiangping.

Just as he watched Li Chejing form incantations in preparation for his breakthrough, Lu Jiangxian suddenly felt as if needles were pricking his back. From afar, he sensed a majestic aura rapidly approaching, flying toward the Great Li Mountains.

Comparing their respective strengths, Lu Jiangxian decisively followed his survival instincts. His divine sense receded into the mirror like an ebbing tide. He even ceased absorbing the moonlight shining through the skylight, lying like a lifeless object in the hidden room of the ancestral hall, exactly like an ordinary bronze mirror.

In just a few short breaths, a streak of azure light flashed across the sky above the Li residence. Sensing the aura moving away, Lu Jiangxian was just about to heave a long sigh of relief when he saw the streak of light weightlessly turn around and slowly come to a hover above the Li family compound.

---

Si Yuanbai had just emerged from his secluded cultivation on Qingsui Peak, his cultivation having progressed yet again. Intending to go into the Great Li Mountains to find trouble with that pig demon, he stepped onto his flying shuttle and headed south along Guli Road.

_That great war severed the spiritual mechanisms at the northern foot of the Great Li Mountains, throwing the earth veins into utter chaos. Now, three hundred years have passed. The earth veins and spiritual mechanisms are slowly recovering, and quite a few demonic creatures have also managed to cultivate to a certain level._

Nearing the Great Li Mountains, Si Yuanbai realized that there were actually quite a few villages at the foot of the mountain. Especially along the banks of that unnamed river, villages were scattered in twos and threes, appearing quite vibrant and full of life.

_The great war wiped the heavens and earth clean, slaughtering all the demonic creatures of this northern foothill and severing the spiritual mechanisms. It has instead become a good dwelling place for these mortals._

Si Yuanbai cast his gaze downward, only to suddenly notice the flashing aura of dharma power within a small village below. He could not help but feel a twinge of curiosity.

Initially thinking it was some minor demon causing mischief, he channeled his dharma eyes to look. To his surprise, under the moonlight sat a handsome youth cross-legged. Not only that, but flanking him were two young men of similar appearance watching over him, who also seemed to possess dharma power.

_How truly bizarre. This youth is breaking through a bottleneck, yet there are no elders by his side to watch over him._

Si Yuanbai lightly swept his spiritual sense over the village but failed to detect any other fluctuations of dharma power. Taken aback for a moment, he clapped his hands and laughed.

"Perhaps he is the illegitimate child of some fellow Daoist's amorous affair."

Down below, Li Chejing remained completely oblivious. Instead, a stroke of inspiration hit him.

As he continuously attempted to condense the wheel, the various major and minor confusions within the incantation suddenly became clear. Streams of dharma power rapidly gathered within the Juque pavilion, and a crystal-clear spiritual wheel began to condense at a flying pace, his proficiency making it seem as if he had practiced it thousands of times.

"What a fine youth!"

Si Yuanbai stroked his beard and praised softly.

The path of immortal cultivation is fraught with danger and difficulty. A spiritual aperture is merely the foundation for entry; one must also rely on karmic fortune, comprehension, and willpower, and even more so on one's family background, sect, and cultivation arts.

This youth's comprehension and innate talent were both quite good, and his cultivation art was profound. Yet, in this mortal village deep in the mountains, how much cultivation resources could he possibly utilize? How many secret incantations and texts could he obtain? In the end, he would achieve no more than Qi Refinement, turning to ash and dust after a mere two hundred years.

The road to immortality is long and arduous. How many heroes have broken their halberds and sunk into the sand? How many brilliant talents have perished along with their entire clans?

Slowly lowering his flying shuttle, Si Yuanbai formed an incantation to cast an invisibility art. Sitting upon the artificial rockery in the courtyard, he leisurely observed the people below.

At that moment, he saw an old mortal man walk in from outside the courtyard.

One of the young men acting as a dharma protector called out, "Father."

Hearing this, Si Yuanbai's expression instantly turned peculiar. Those possessing a spiritual aperture were one in a thousand, let alone a single family producing three. Could these truly be the seed of that old mortal man?

Given their rich and pure dharma power, they undoubtedly possessed a profound art—they had to be the illegitimate children of some fellow Daoist who, despite the rarity of having offspring, lacked the courage to bring them home and had no choice but to raise them in this village to begin their cultivation.

_However, for all three of these children to possess a spiritual aperture, this fellow Daoist's cultivation must be at Foundation Establishment or above._

Although a higher cultivation base made it harder to bear children, the probability of their offspring possessing a spiritual aperture increased significantly.

A Qi Refinement cultivator might reach a one or two in ten chance, but after Foundation Establishment, it rose to fifty percent. Since all three of these children possessed a spiritual aperture, their father was presumably a Foundation Establishment cultivator.

As for the Purple Mansion realm? Excluding the patriarchs of the various immortal sects, the entirety of the Yue State could not even scrape together enough to count on two hands. Why would a dignified Purple Mansion exalted one come to make do in this tiny village? Even if they had illegitimate children, they could just bring them home—who would dare utter a word of dissent? They would have to be worshipped like little ancestors.

He then watched as the youth below retracted his arts and spoke with a face full of joy, "Father, I have broken through!"

The Li family was naturally awash in joy. Up on the rockery, however, Si Yuanbai gazed at him, a thought stirring in his mind as he pondered.

_This stretch from Guli Road to the Great Li Mountains has many mortals, yet due to that great war, it has been without a guarding cultivator for three hundred years. Now that the earth veins are returning to their positions and the spiritual mechanisms are gradually recovering, this land must be brought back under the sect's jurisdiction soon._

_Why not use these people as sect vassals to guard this place? Borrowing the prestige of that Foundation Establishment senior, they should actually be able to keep these demonic creatures in check._

The Azure Pond Sect controlled the five southern commanderies of the Yue State, naturally possessing its own system of governance. The sect only dispatched disciples to govern at the city level; for the rest, they utilized the intricate web of intermarriages of cultivation clans to control the lower rungs.

The sect used pills and dharma artifacts to coerce these clans into cultivating raw materials and providing vast amounts of low-cultivation manpower. Then, year after year, they would harvest the clans' most outstanding disciples to enrich their own ranks. This was an excellent method universally acknowledged throughout the cultivation world.

The Azure Pond Sect had been employing it for over five hundred years since its founding.

_Along the way, I can also forge a good karmic tie with that Foundation Establishment cultivator._

With this in mind, Si Yuanbai formed an incantation, revealing his figure as he floated lightly down into the courtyard.

Li Chejing was currently basking in joy, experiencing the sensation of the Heavenly Circuit Wheel's ceaselessly surging dharma power, when he suddenly saw a figure draped in azure clothes materialize out of thin air in the courtyard.

The man was dressed in azure, appearing to be in his thirties or forties. He possessed a handsome appearance and a gentle smile, with a radiantly glowing precious sword equipped at his waist, giving him an ethereal, otherworldly air.

Li Chejing and the others immediately acted as if facing a mortal enemy, swiftly moving to protect Li Mutian behind them.

Li Tongya shouted in a low voice, "Who might you be, sir?"

[^1]: Located on the upper abdomen, just below the sternum. Juque (巨阙) translates to "Great Gate" or "Great Tower."
[^2]: Li Chejing circulates his qi through the following apertures:

    * **Guanyuan (Gate of Origin)** and **Shimen (Stone Gate)** are located on the lower abdomen, strongly associated with the lower dantian and the gathering of foundational essence.
    * **Shenque (Spirit Gate)** is located exactly at the navel.
    * **Mingmen (Gate of Life)** is located on the lower back, directly opposite the navel.
    
    By cycling energy through the front of the body (the Conception Vessel) and the back (the Governing Vessel), the cultivator completes a foundational internal circuit before driving that condensed power upward into the Juque to form the new spiritual wheel.
