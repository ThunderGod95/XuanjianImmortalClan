# Chapter 45: Stalling

Li Tongya stared at Wan Tiancang, feeling incredibly awkward. If his family truly possessed talismans of the Qi Refinement or Foundation Establishment level, he would certainly consider trading them for an array inheritance.

However, the white light that had saved Wan Xiaohua was no talisman at all; it was an attack unleashed by the dharma mirror when it encountered an enemy. This left Li Tongya with no way to explain.

"Brother Wan, it is not that I am unwilling to trade, but it is simply impossible..." Li Tongya began, sighing inwardly as he uttered a sentence even he didn't quite believe.

Wan Tiancang naturally refused to believe him. He frowned and began to argue his case, detailing the benefits and risks, while feeling confused himself.

_This is clearly a deal where both sides win. Why is Li Tongya being so vague and acting so uninterested?_

Seeing Li Tongya’s troubled expression, Wan Tiancang suddenly had a realization and spoke with solemn conviction, "Brother Tongya, rest assured! Within the array inheritance, only the Earth-Locking Huaqian[^1] Array on our Mount Huaqian is vital to our clan's security and cannot be shared. The other ten Fetal Breathing arrays and three Qi Refinement arrays will be handed over to your noble clan in their entirety, without a single detail missing or altered!"

Hearing the offer put in such terms, Li Tongya was deeply tempted.

He could only resort to a stalling tactic, nodding as he said, "I must still discuss this with my father and younger brother before I can give you an answer, Brother Wan."

"Of course, of course!" Wan Tiancang nodded repeatedly, feeling that the matter was as good as settled. He let out a sigh of relief and added, "A matter of this magnitude naturally requires your father's consent. I shall await your good news, Brother Tongya!"

After a few more exchanges, Wan Tiancang took his leave.

---

Atop Mount Lijing.

_This Wan Tiancang certainly has a silver tongue._

Lu Jiangxian's divine sense had observed their actions clearly, his mind whirling with a myriad of thoughts.

_The power of that Supreme Yin Profound Light is equivalent to a single attack concentrated from the entire cultivation of a peak Jade Capital Wheel cultivator. It seems even a cultivator who has just entered the Qi Refinement stage would suffer a heavy blow if hit directly. No wonder Wan Tiancang is seeking it._

As for the Li family, Lu Jiangxian was quite satisfied. The entire family had entered the immortal path through his Profound Pearl Talisman Seeds. Each person's seed condensed their entire cultivation; it could be said that their rise or fall rested entirely upon Lu Jiangxian's whim. This gave him a great sense of security.

Every time a powerful aura flew over the Great Li Mountains, Lu Jiangxian would honestly disguise himself as an ordinary mirror, hiding in this remote forest. He had no desire to become a tool in the hands of others. Who knew what methods those of the Purple Mansion or Golden Core stages possessed? They might very well be able to deal with an existence like him, who was akin to an artifact spirit.

While it was possible he would recover faster in the hands of those powerful cultivators, nothing could compare to the comfort of lying low and developing in these mountains. Every Profound Pearl Talisman Seed he planted served as a feeler, transmitting information and searching for treasures. Each breakthrough made by these people added to his own dharma power and divine sense.

_How could being a servant ever be as comfortable as being the master!_

Lu Jiangxian murmured to himself, feeling deeply satisfied as he watched the Li family thrive under the protection of the seeds he had nurtured.

Yet, thinking from another perspective, if he were a member of the Li family and his entire cultivation were held in the hands of a sentient artifact, Lu Jiangxian would absolutely never tolerate it. He would find every possible way to gain control.

The Li family members were clever and capable, but they were also suspicious and calculating. Therefore, Lu Jiangxian never displayed any sapience, instead acting as if he moved purely on instinct.

As for the Wan family's array inheritance, Lu Jiangxian was also quite tempted. He wondered what the effect would be if he used his mirror body as a medium to set up an array.

_But how can I let the Li family obtain this inheritance..._

---

"An array inheritance?" Li Xiangping frowned, looking at Li Tongya as he shook his head. "Our family has no talismans to give him. But this array inheritance is truly tempting. I am quite envious myself."

Li Xiangping was also moved. He slapped his thigh and stood up, pulling Li Tongya toward the back courtyard.

"Let us go ask Father."

Li Mutian was currently sweeping the small room in the back courtyard. The old man had few hobbies; he did not smoke or gamble. Usually, he would spend his time playing with his grandson, chatting with Tian Shoushui, or wandering around the room where the mirror was kept, as if he feared it might suddenly fly out of the skylight if he weren't looking.

Li Xuanxuan was cultivating in the side room. With the help of the lunar essence on the mirror's surface, the child had already condensed seventy strands of lunar essence. He was only a step slower than Li Chejing had been at his age, which brought great joy to Lady Ren and the elders.

After hearing Li Xiangping's report, Li Mutian set down his broom and gazed at the mirror on the stone pedestal.

"This will be difficult to manage," he said in a deep voice.

Seeing the regret on his two sons' faces, Li Mutian narrowed his eyes and continued, "Regarding that demon killed by the mirror's white light recently... what do you two think of it?"

Li Tongya and his brother paused, sinking into deep thought.

"Such a spiritual vessel is born with the destiny to slay evil and eliminate demons. Perhaps because that demon came too close, it was slain by the mirror," Li Tongya offered after a few moments of reflection.

"That is a reasonable explanation," Li Mutian nodded and said in a low voice. "If we are to help Wan Tiancang, we could tell him that the talisman must be used by a member of our family. Then, we could use the dharma mirror to launch a sneak attack on Ji Dengqi from afar. However, Ji Dengqi is neither evil nor a demon. How could we induce the mirror to attack him?"

Li Xiangping remained silent for a long time before suddenly speaking, "The path of immortal cultivation begins at the Profound Scenery stage. Alchemical refinement, artifact forging, talisman drawing, and array setting—even storage bags—all require the birth of spiritual sense at the Jade Capital Wheel to be used. Perhaps once we attain the Jade Capital Wheel, we can unravel some of the mysteries surrounding the dharma mirror and borrow its power to slay enemies."

"True," Li Tongya nodded. Calculating the time, he said, "The Wan family's spirit rice has only just matured one crop. It will be at least two to three years before the next harvest, which is when Ji Dengqi will strike again. We have plenty of time."

Li Mutian sat on a stone stool, picked up the teapot, and filled the cups before him.

"We shall stall for now," he said slowly. "We will wait until our Li family produces a Jade Capital Wheel cultivator and our strength is comparable to the Wan family's before resolving these issues. Until then, let Ji Dengqi keep the pressure on the Wan family for us."

"Understood."

Li Xiangping took a small sip of clear tea and asked Li Tongya, "How is your progress, Second Brother?"

"I have already touched the edges of the Azure Essence Wheel. After a few months of breathing, I will wait for a day when the weather is auspicious and the yang is rising to attempt a breakthrough." Li Tongya smiled lightly, looking at the water-like moonlight and the pale white lunar essence flowing over the mirror. "This Azure Essence Wheel is much easier than the Bearing Brightness Wheel."

Li Xiangping shook his head with a smile and said seriously, "In a few months, the Azure Pond Sect emissary will arrive to collect the tribute. We should also consult the Wan family on the protocols for that."

[^1]: The name Huaqian (华芊) translates to "Verdant Splendor" or "Flourishing and Lush."
