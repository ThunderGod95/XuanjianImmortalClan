# Chapter 33: Xiao Yuansi

"Qingsui Peak of the Azure Pond Sect?" Li Tongya and Li Xiangping immediately exchanged bewildered glances.

After a few breaths of hesitation, Li Tongya said, "An envoy from the immortal sect... it must be related to Jing'er."

Li Xiangping hurriedly stood up, leading the group down the mountain. To their surprise, just as they stepped out of the courtyard gate, they spotted a cultivator dressed in a moon-white robe, a string of brocade pouches hanging from his waist.

The cultivator possessed a handsome countenance and an otherworldly temperament. He stood quietly before the courtyard, gazing at the group with a smile.

"Xiao Yuansi of Qingsui Peak, paying his respects to fellow Daoists."

Xiao Yuansi lightly cupped his hands, a smile gracing his face.

Not daring to act presumptuously, Li Xiangping hastily led the men behind him in a deep bow, "Li Xiangping of the Li family, paying respects to the envoy of the Azure Pond Sect."

Xiao Yuansi slightly turned his body to avoid accepting the full bow and said with a smile, "You are all the family members of my junior brother; there is no need for such grand formalities. I have come today merely representing myself, not as some envoy of the immortal sect."

The group voiced their agreement in unison, and Li Xiangping respectfully said, "Immortal Master, please come in."

Before this high-level cultivator of the Azure Pond Sect, Li Tongya and the others were no different from mortals. Thus, their address of 'Immortal Master' came from genuine sincerity.

Xiao Yuansi stepped into the courtyard, found a seat, and said softly, "I was originally tasked by the sect with an important matter to subdue a demon in Lixia commandery. However, my junior brother missed his family dearly, so I had him pen a letter to bring along to the Great Li Mountains."

"Over the past few days, I was locked in a chase with that demon, delaying me for many hours. I only managed to slay it yesterday. Thus, I arrived uninvited; I hope everyone will not take offense."

"We wouldn't dare, we wouldn't dare! The Immortal Master traveled countless miles to deliver a family letter; the Li family is eternally grateful!" Li Xiangping replied respectfully. Seeing Xiao Yuansi hand over a cyan bamboo tube the thickness of a thumb, he hurriedly accepted it.

"You all should read it first!" Xiao Yuansi took the tea offered by a servant, took a gentle sip, and smiled.

Li Xiangping and the others nodded quickly. Li Tongya used his thumb to lightly rub away the wax seal on the bamboo tube. Tipping it into his palm, a curled, pale-white letter slid out, which he quickly unrolled to read.

_Jing bows his head. It has been over a year since I left home. I do not know if my brothers and father are still healthy. Jing'er is doing well in the immortal sect. I have now successfully cultivated the Azure Essence Wheel, and the Jade Capital is in sight. I have also practiced sword arts, making great strides in my cultivation..._

_My Senior Brother, Xiao Yuansi, has reached the seventh level of Qi Refinement. He excels most at alchemy and possesses a gentle, sincere nature. If there are matters the family cannot decide upon, you may seek his aid. If you need anything, do not hesitate to ask him; when he returns, your son will compensate him within the sect..._

_Accompanying this letter is a jade slip containing a sword art called the ⟨Profound Water Sword Art⟩. The dharma spells I have learned are all inheritances of the sect and cannot be shared with outsiders. Only this Qi Refinement sword art was gifted by Senior Brother. I asked him, and he permitted me to send it home. This sword art is not difficult to master; the family can practice it for self-defense._

_I have heard that the Metal Tang Gate has provoked numerous border skirmishes; the family should endure it for now. I have already asked Senior Brother to greet the stationed cultivators in Lixia commandery on our behalf. If an emergency arises at home, you can use the jade seal to call for help..._

_I am very free and comfortable in the immortal sect, so there is no need to worry. Cultivating here yields twice the result with half the effort; it is far superior to the Great Li Mountains. Has any major event occurred at home? Is Xuanxuan still well-behaved? Is there... anything unusual at home? I eagerly await your reply._

After reading the letter, an aching sorrow welled up in Li Xiangping's heart. His eyes slightly red, he cupped his hands toward Xiao Yuansi and said, "Pardon my rudeness. I have kept the Immortal Master waiting."

Xiao Yuansi waved his hand with a smile and placed a jade slip on the redwood table. "No need for formalities, it is only human nature. This is the item my junior brother asked me to bring along."

Seeing Li Tongya step forward to accept the jade slip, Xiao Yuansi asked softly, "If the family has any difficulties or doubts, feel free to bring them up."

Li Tongya and Li Xiangping exchanged a look and spoke in unison, "We do have some doubts. Immortal Master, please follow us."

The group dismissed their attendants and walked upward along the stone path of the mountain courtyard. Before long, they arrived in front of a lush, leafy banyan tree. Gently stepping around the banyan, they found a small tree bearing five fruits standing right behind it.

"Oh?"

Xiao Yuansi lowered his gaze. Weaving a hand seal with his fingers, his hands immediately emitted a jade-like white glow. Only then did he reach out to touch the fruit.

He carefully examined the leaves for a few breaths before explaining, "This spirit root is called the Serpentine Wyrm Fruit. A single plant should bear six fruits. Going upwards along this spirit root, the fruits higher up mature earlier. Only when the first fruit matures and is plucked will the one below it continue to ripen."

"The time between the maturation of the first and second fruit is about five years. You could also view it as bearing one fruit every five years. Looking at the quality of this highest fruit, it has been over four years since the previous one was plucked. This one is also nearing maturity."

Xiao Yuansi brushed aside the lowest leaves of the Serpentine Wyrm Fruit, revealing a tiny, tender green fruit—it was indeed the newly grown sixth fruit.

"May I ask what use this fruit has?" Li Tongya listened attentively and inquired respectfully.

"Our Azure Pond Sect also possesses over a dozen of these plants. It takes centuries for them to bear a golden fruit that spawns a new sapling. Most of these crimson Serpentine Wyrm Fruits are used to refine Serpentine Essence Pills, which are rewarded to meritorious disciples in the Fetal Breathing and Qi Refinement stages to accelerate the speed at which they nourish their wheels and refine their qi."

Li Tongya and Li Xiangping exchanged helpless glances. The Li family was utterly destitute at the moment; where would they find an alchemist? Despite their years of cultivation, neither of them had ever even consumed a pill.

Seeing the troubled expressions on their faces, Xiao Yuansi chuckled lightly, "If this fruit is eaten raw, it would likely only shorten the wheel-nourishing and qi-refining time for a single person."

Noticing that the two men looked as if they wanted to say something but hesitated, Xiao Yuansi suddenly understood and offered, "For this current fruit, I can first refine a furnace of pills for you. With my current mastery of the Dao of Alchemy, a furnace of Serpentine Wyrm Pills should yield about seven to eight pills. However, according to the rules, I will give you six pills, and the surplus will belong to me."

Li Tongya and Li Xiangping naturally voiced their agreement repeatedly, thanking him with great joy. Xiao Yuansi nodded and tapped the brocade pouch at his waist. Instantly, a jade box materialized in his right hand.

Xiao Yuansi gathered a strand of dharma power in his hand, tapping the stem of the fruit. Catching it with his dharma power, the Serpentine Wyrm Fruit fell gently into the jade box.

"Aside from certain special herbs and spirit fruits, most must be stored in jade vessels so as not to damage their spirituality. Furthermore, they must be harvested using dharma power to prevent injuring the fruit," Xiao Yuansi explained softly as he harvested the fruit.

"I will open the furnace to refine the pills tomorrow!"

He lightly tapped his brocade pouch, and the jade box vanished from his hands, leaving Li Tongya and Li Xiangping burning with envy.

"Do you have any other important matters?" Xiao Yuansi asked with a smile after stowing away the jade box.

"Thank you, Immortal Master!"

Li Xiangping and Li Tongya first expressed their gratitude before taking out an animal skin scroll from a sleeve and presenting it to Xiao Yuansi with both hands.

Xiao Yuansi took the animal skin scroll, untied the binding white cord, and gently unfurled it with his right hand.

"The Miasma Obscuring Array?"
