# Chapter 43: Slaying

In the small courtyard atop Mount Lijing, a grayish-blue mirror was radiantly emitting white light, as boiling white lunar essence gushed from the mirror's surface like water.

The moment Wan Xiaohua appeared, Li Xiangping and the others entered his divine sense's range. The Supreme Yin Profound Light within the mirror was already eager to strike. Lu Jiangxian suppressed the urge to act, wanting to see what kind of trump cards this Jade Capital Wheel cultivator possessed.

_Seems... not very impressive?_

Watching Wan Xiaohua suffer consecutive setbacks, surrounded by perils, and being forcibly driven by the demon to use a pale blue talisman, sprinting wildly toward the foot of Mount Lijing with a water shield overhead, Lu Jiangxian lost the mood to watch the spectacle.

He silently channeled his dharma technique and thought to himself, _I will use this demon to test the waters!_

With a thought from Lu Jiangxian, the twelve talismanic scripts along the mirror's edge lit up one by one. The grayish-blue mirror, enveloped in milky-white lunar essence, slowly levitated from the stone pedestal, adjusting its angle little by little.

"Father..."

Under the shocked gazes of Li Tongya and Li Mutian, the white lunar essence suddenly spewed forth, shattering the skylight of the small cabin. It tore through the sky like a brilliant shooting star, leaving behind a floor of crushed stone and scattered gray roof tiles.

Having fired the Supreme Yin Profound Light, this grayish-blue mirror finally settled down slowly, dispersing the white lunar essence and descending back onto the stone pedestal.

Looking at the shattered tiles and dust covering the floor, Li Tongya did not know what to think for a moment.

Li Mutian coughed twice from the dust, waved his hand at him, and said, "It most likely has to do with that demon... Hurry and see how your brother is doing!"

Only then did Li Tongya snap out of it as if waking from a dream and took off running, pondering in his heart, _How else could it be? With such a massive disturbance, let alone a Fetal Breathing stage demon, even a Qi Refinement stage demon would have been blasted into meat paste._

Wan Xiaohua slapped a divine movement talisman onto his leg. As he sprinted wildly toward the direction of Mount Lijing that Li Xiangping had previously pointed out, he was filled with boundless regret.

_This is a massive loss. One water shield talisman cost another half a spirit stone._

A foul wind rushed at his ears. Wan Xiaohua swept his spiritual sense behind him, and a small white shield had already materialized at his back. Borrowing the force of the blow, he once again widened the distance between himself and the demon.

"This..."

Wan Xiaohua looked at the mountain ahead, originally intending to estimate the distance, but he never expected a brilliant white light to suddenly rise from the mountain peak. It took a turn in the air, dragging a long tail of flame, and hurtled straight toward him.

"Motherf—"

Wan Xiaohua almost spat out a mouthful of old blood, hastily dodging to the side, yet the white light ignored him completely and surged past.

Terrified, he collapsed to the ground, watching as this streak of white light passed straight through the demon's neck, flying off into the distance as effortlessly as if it were piercing a thin sheet of paper, leaving behind a headless wolf corpse leaping high into the air.

"Boom!"

The giant wolf's corpse crashed heavily to the ground. Behind it, the white light felled a swath of large trees before disappearing into the distance, shaking the snow from the entire dense forest to the ground simultaneously and nearly burying Wan Xiaohua in snow.

"Cough, cough, cough."

In a sorry state, Wan Xiaohua spat out a few mouthfuls of dust-filled snow, staring dumbfounded at the hill-sized demon corpse.

"Patriarch, Patriarch?!"

Wan Tiancang's shouts drew closer from afar. He hurriedly pulled Wan Xiaohua from the snow and repeatedly asked, "Patriarch! How are you?!"

Wan Xiaohua stared blankly at Wan Tiancang, muttering to himself, "Using a butcher's knife to kill a chicken..."

Wan Tiancang's face was covered in sweat. He took the rattan whip, still clinging with bits of snow, from his robes and said in a deep voice, "I was terrified of losing the treasure, so I remained hidden in the snow and did not leave. Once the wolf demon went to chase you, Patriarch, I retrieved this dharma artifact."

"Good, good."

Seeing the dharma artifact, Wan Xiaohua was jolted awake. He rallied his spirits, stowed the treasure into his robes, hastily scrambled up, and dashed toward the wolf corpse.

Seeing that thick frost had formed at the corpse's neck, preventing even a single drop of wolf blood from spilling out, Wan Xiaohua hastily formed seals and struck the wolf corpse's joints and major apertures, sealing its spiritual nature.

Wan Xiaohua laughed, stroking the wolf's back, which was as massive as a yellow ox, and spoke with a beaming smile, "This wolf corpse is a fine item; we absolutely cannot let its spiritual nature dissipate."

"Patriarch, that demon... most likely belongs to them." Watching Wan Xiaohua's overjoyed demeanor, Wan Tiancang carefully reminded him.

Wan Xiaohua's joyful expression instantly froze. He cast a low glance at Wan Tiancang and said in a deep voice, "I know."

"Patriarch Wan!"

Wan Tiancang and Wan Xiaohua were standing there staring wide-eyed at each other when they heard a loud shout. The two hastily turned their heads to look, only to see Li Xiangping and Li Tongya arriving with Chen Erniu and a group of village guards.

"Many thanks to Patriarch Wan for coming to our aid!"

Li Xiangping smiled and cupped his hands in greeting. Chen Erniu caught his look and hastily directed the village guards under him to move the corpse.

"I have made a fool of myself." Wan Xiaohua smiled bitterly and shook the snow from his body before asking, "I wonder about that white light that saved me..."

Hearing this, Li Tongya quickly took a step forward and spoke, "My father still possessed a few talismans he crafted himself in his youth. Seeing the Immortal Master come from afar to aid us, the old man feared the demon might harm you, so he ordered us to use one."

"So that is how it is!"

Wan Xiaohua let out a long breath. Li Tongya's answer was not far from his own estimation, and he spoke with some gratitude, "Then I must thank your esteemed father."

"It is we who must thank the Wan family!"

The men exchanged pleasantries for a moment before Wan Xiaohua pointed at the demon's corpse and smiled, "I do not know how your noble clan intends to handle this item. This wolf demon corpse is not easy to preserve, yet it is a treasure trove. The bones and blood can be used to make ink and refine medicines, and the pelt can be used to craft talismans and garments. It truly is a fine item."

Li Tongya and Li Xiangping exchanged a glance and smiled bitterly, "Our family's foundations are shallow; crafting talismans and refining medicines are exceedingly difficult for us, so much of this will be of no use. Since Patriarch Wan can enter and exit the market, it would be better to entrust it to your noble clan to sell."

"This wolf demon's size is quite massive and somewhat extraordinary; it has likely consumed some precious medicine. I will not deceive you; sold piecemeal in the market, it could fetch two or three spirit stones."

Wan Xiaohua pondered for a few breaths, glancing at the two men who were listening intently, and continued, "I still have one and a half spirit stones on my person, which I will give to you first. As for the rest, I will use some other items to offset the cost."

"Have you two ever read the ⟦Essentials of Miscellaneous Cultivation⟧?"

Seeing Li Xiangping and Li Tongya shake their heads, Wan Xiaohua paused inwardly and thought, _Is this Li Mutian really a Foundation Establishment cultivator? I fear there is a hidden truth here..._

On the surface, however, he smiled and said, "This manual records the dharna arts fundamentals and cultivation insights of a Nascent Spirit Wheel rogue cultivator. I picked it up in the market for the price of one or two spirit stones. It contains a few minor dharma techniques such as the ⟨Divine Movement Art⟩ and the ⟨Spirit-Sealing Art⟩. How about I use it to offset the rest of the cost to your noble clan?"

Li Xiangping looked at Wan Xiaohua's bright smile, rubbed his chin, and thought to himself, _We have no use for this wolf demon corpse either, and it is said to be difficult to preserve. We might as well trade it to this Wan Xiaohua. We might lose out a little, but everyone gets what they need._

Thus, Li Xiangping smiled and said, "We defer entirely to Patriarch Wan's judgment."

"Good, good!" Wan Xiaohua was instantly overjoyed and added, "Please have your noble clan send men to transport this corpse to the border between our two families; I will send people over to collect it."

"Agreed."

The two parties chatted for a few more moments. Li Xiangping glanced at the sky and cupped his hands, "Would Patriarch Wan like to visit Mount Lijing for a rest?"

"There is no need. The clan has many pressing matters, so I will return first. If there is anything in the future, just instruct Tiancang to write a letter!"

Wan Xiaohua hastily refused. Mount Lijing had an array formation. Although the two families currently seemed to have a harmonious atmosphere, who knew if he would be able to come back out once he went in? Understanding that Li Xiangping was seeing him off, he exchanged a few polite words and took his leave.
