# Chapter 25: Fruit

"Fruit?"

Li Xiangping raised his head, looking with confusion at Li Qiuyang, who sat cross-legged.

"Yes..."

Li Qiuyang appeared somewhat anxious. His hands tightly gripped the hem of his clothes. After cultivating for a few nights, he had condensed a thread of Fetal Breathing spiritual energy. Feeling his speed did not match what was described in the scroll, and terrified he was cultivating the method incorrectly, he hurriedly brought the manual to ask Li Xiangping.

Hearing Li Qiuyang's cultivation speed, Li Xiangping was so startled that he brought over the Azure Essence Wheel-Nurturing Method to look at it again and again, pondering over it carefully several times.

After a full cup of tea's time, Li Xiangping finally looked up, asking him if he had eaten anything strange.

Thinking carefully, Li Qiuyang suddenly remembered a major event and quickly replied, "A few years ago, a few of my friends insisted on a test of courage, agreeing to climb the rear mountain. In the end, only I went up the mountain. I lost sight of my friends along the way and unknowingly got lost."

"As I walked, I saw a small, emerald-green tree. Upon it hung six or seven bright red fruits. They were radiant and looked incredibly delicious."

"I truly could not resist. As if possessed by ghosts, I picked a fruit and ate it. I wandered down the mountain in a daze, and after returning, I slept for three days. My father was so frantic that he sought out numerous physicians to examine me.""

Listening to Li Qiuyang finish his tale, Li Xiangping was left momentarily speechless.

He patted Li Qiuyang's shoulder and said, "Looking at it now, it will only take a little over a year for you to condense the Profound Scenery Wheel."

Seeing Li Qiuyang's face full of hope, Li Xiangping continued, "However, there is still an urgent matter."

Li Qiuyang was momentarily taken aback, looking up at Li Xiangping.

"Take us up the mountain to search for that spiritual root."

---

Li Tongya returned to the Lijing courtyard. Just as he stepped over the stone steps before the door, he saw Liu Rouxuan sitting obediently in the yard, resting her chin on her hands.

She was blankly counting the fruit seedlings in the spirit field for amusement.

Seeing Li Tongya enter through the gate, Liu Rouxuan shot up from the stone stool. Lowering her brows, she looked at Li Tongya and spoke timidly, "Immortal Master."

"There is no need to be so polite." Li Tongya waved his hand. His gaze quickly swept past Liu Rouxuan's delicate features, and he smiled, "I am only six or seven years older than you; calling me Tongya will suffice."

Seeing that Li Tongya appeared easy to talk to, Liu Rouxuan relaxed significantly. She thought for a moment before saying, "Brother Tongya."

Li Tongya nodded. He rose and retrieved a wooden slip from a wooden rack in the room, telling Liu Rouxuan, "This is the incantation for the Fetal Breathing realm, called the Azure Essence Wheel-Nurturing Method. Memorize it in the courtyard first, and firmly remember to never pass it on to outsiders."

"Yes!"

Liu Rouxuan responded with pleasant surprise. She respectfully received the wooden slip with both hands, carefully cradling it in her embrace.

"Meals will be delivered every day during the hours of Chen and Shen[^1]. If there is anything you need, you may tell the person to bring it over."

Li Tongya took a teapot, speaking as he poured. Filling two small cups, he continued, "I am not usually in this courtyard, but I will come during the hours of Chen and Shen to cast spirit rain upon the spirit field. If you have any questions regarding your cultivation, feel free to ask."

Watching Liu Rouxuan nod repeatedly, Li Tongya smiled and asked softly, "Have your household utensils and clothing been delivered?"

"They have already been delivered," Liu Rouxuan replied.

"Then you may tidy up the east room and settle in. Cultivate diligently in your daily life, and do not go out until you condense the Profound Scenery Wheel. Have you studied books before?" Li Tongya suddenly asked.

"I read a little when I was young, and I recognize some characters."

"Then there is no need for me to teach you one by one. Read through it entirely first, and come to me if you have any questions."

Liu Rouxuan agreed repeatedly, holding the wooden slip as she began reading word by word.

Watching Liu Rouxuan bury her head into reading the Azure Essence Wheel-Nurturing Method, Li Tongya sipped his tea, his thoughts wandering.

_This child has come to Lijing, unfamiliar with the people and the place. Being confined to this courtyard, I need not worry about the cultivation method leaking out in these coming years._

Just as he was thinking, a hearty laugh rang out, and someone pushed open the courtyard door.

"Third Brother, why have you come?" Li Tongya asked in slight surprise, only to see Li Qiuyang following behind Li Xiangping with an anxious expression.

Li Xiangping recounted the matter of the spirit root. Naturally, Li Tongya was highly astonished, silently taking extra note of this Li Qiuyang. After the two conferred, they took Li Yesheng and several strong men from the village and headed up the rear mountain.

The season had already entered late autumn. The rear mountain was desolate and sparse, leaves falling in profusion. The wild beasts had grown fat and sturdy, lying dormant in preparation for winter.

Having the abundant and vibrant reed marshes, the people of Lijing had never held much interest in the rear mountain. Fish and meat were provided by the marshes, so why risk their lives fighting wild beasts in the deep mountains?

Therefore, generations of Lijing villagers only relied on the few patches of woods at the foot of the rear mountain to chop firewood, using Loropetalum or Serissa[^2] to cook. These short shrubs grew quickly and were easy to gather. Only when building houses did they go up the mountain to log timber.

As a result, the paths on the rear mountain were overgrown with thorns and covered in vines. Li Qiuyang could not clearly remember the exact route, so the village men hacked a path with machetes, while Li Xiangping and Li Tongya followed behind.

Li Xiangping, holding Li Qiuyang's hand, turned his head to look at Li Tongya, "Second Brother, I have some doubts."

"What is it?"

"You mentioned that Si Yuanbai drew boundaries for our family, and beyond these borders lies the territory of others. Before our Li family rose to power, that family only needed to send a single Fetal Breathing cultivator here. Who would dare disobey their orders?"

"Then why has our Li family toiled in this land for two hundred years without a single cultivator willing to come? Do they truly disdain these mortals so much?"

Li Tongya stroked his chin, his expression grave as he spoke, "I have also pondered this matter. From what Si Yuanbai implied, this land was completely devoid of spiritual mechanisms for centuries, and thus lacked any cultivation resources. Consequently, no cultivator was willing to waste their time here."

"Secondly, bordering this place is the Great Li Mountains. The mountain ranges stretch on endlessly. Who knows how many demons and ghosts lurk within them? It is far less comfortable than the commandery city."

Li Xiangping nodded in agreement, his face looking somewhat grim. He lowered his voice and spoke to Li Tongya, "Brother, there is an even worse possibility."

"Although the Azure Pond Sect is said to be incomparably powerful, it only occupies a quarter of Yue State. Are there no formidable enemies surrounding it? I fear that our Li family resides right on the edge of the Azure Pond Sect's sphere of influence!"

"When two villages fight over water, naturally no one is willing to plant crops right on the borders; the principle is the same here. According to Xu Wenshan, further south is Wu State. There is constant friction between Wu and Yue, and the relations between the immortal sects behind them are likely just as poor!"

Li Tongya's heart skipped a beat, his expression turning ugly as he spoke,"That was an oversight on my part. Tomorrow, we will send some men to carefully scout the areas surrounding the Guli Road. Ascertaining our Li family's situation is the most pressing matter."

"I only hope our Li family is not just crops casually planted on the borders by the Azure Pond Sect," Li Xiangping said with a bitter smile.

Just then, they heard Li Yesheng and several village men shouting from ahead.

"Immortal Master! What a massive serpent!"
