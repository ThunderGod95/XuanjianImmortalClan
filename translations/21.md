# Chapter 21: Consolidating Power

Li Xiangping watched until Si Yuanbai disappeared over the horizon atop his flying shuttle before finally standing up. He picked up the wooden slip from the table, untied the cloth strip binding it, and unrolled it to read.

"⟨Azure Essence Wheel-Nurturing Method⟩, First Grade."

He skimmed through it briefly; the technique was a cultivation method for the Fetal Breathing realm.

As he read on with a furrowed brow, he heard Li Tongya let out a soft sound of surprise, "This breathing method only requires daily qi refining; it does not require refining lunar essence?"

"Naturally, it cannot compare to the mir... to our family's technique."

Li Xiangping had just been about to mention the mirror, but fearing that Si Yuanbai might return as suddenly as he had left, he quickly swallowed the thought.

Shifting the subject, he asked, "Is that Si Yuanbai truly a Peak Master of the Azure Pond Sect? For Jing'er to just leave with him like that seems somewhat hasty."

Li Tongya smiled bitterly and replied, "What other choice did we have? If he were some evil cultivator, he could have just abducted him. Why would he waste his breath on us and even leave behind techniques and tokens of proof?"

Li Xiangping nodded. Picking up the second wooden slip from the table, he unrolled it and read, "⟨Heavenly Essence Qi Refinement Incantation⟩, a cultivation method for the Qi Refinement realm, Second Grade."

Li Tongya also picked one up and read aloud to himself, "Secret Missive of Lixia."

After reading a few more lines, joy visibly crept onto his face as he turned to Li Xiangping, "This missive records a great deal of common cultivation knowledge, the distribution of powers in Lixia commandery[^1], and the trading markets of rogue cultivators. This is exactly what we desperately need right now."

Taking the wooden slip to look, Li Xiangping replied thoughtfully, "So cultivation methods are divided into nine grades, with the first grade being the lowest and the ninth being the highest. Techniques, pills, and dharma artifacts correspond to one's realm. I just wonder what grade our family's method is."

Seeing his younger brother engrossed in reading the missive, Li Tongya picked up the pale azure jade seal Si Yuanbai had left behind and silently channeled his dharma power.

«Under Azure Pond's Rule»

Several large, pale golden characters immediately surfaced on the jade seal. Following that, the large characters slowly faded, replaced by a bright white character for "Li" and a tiny map.

"Lijing Village, Lichuankou, Jingyang Village, Lidaokou."[^2]

Li Mutian squinted to identify the locations for a moment before reading out the names of these villages one by one. These four villages were exactly what Si Yuanbai had referred to: the settlements spanning from Guli Road to the foot of the Great Li Mountains.

"With the Azure Pond Immortal Sect as our backing, our Li family now possesses sufficient strength. We no longer need to hide; it is time to openly and righteously cultivate our power! These villages belong to our Li family now."

Li Xiangping, who had his head lowered in study, suddenly looked up and stated calmly, "We shall begin tomorrow night."

---

Chen Erniu woke up bright and early only to see the Li family's tenants weaving through the village from end to end like swimming fish, going door-to-door to gather people.

"Auntie Li! Tell Brother Li that after he finishes in the fields tonight, he should head to the rear of the village. Uncle Tian has an important matter to discuss!"

"Ah! Alright..."

"Brother Liu! The Li family invites you..."

For a time, the village was in a chaotic uproar. Everyone harbored a bellyful of doubts. Having finally endured until dusk, Chen Erniu hastily finished his meal and rushed to the rear of the village.

As one of the premier prominent families in the village, the Li family wielded immense rallying power. Chen Erniu looked up and saw that families big and small had all gathered. Even Xu Wenshan, the middle-aged refugee leader from a few years ago, was standing respectfully behind Li Xiangping.

"Motherfucker, I came too late," Chen Erniu cursed in a low voice.

Nowadays, this Xu Wenshan had also become a tenant of the Li family. With his pleasant words and tactful demeanor, he had gathered over a dozen refugee households to band together for warmth, faintly supplanting Chen Erniu as the head of the tenants. Seeing this made Chen Erniu gnash his teeth in resentment.

Scanning his surroundings, he did not see any sign of Li Mutian. Instead, Li Xiangping stepped forward and spoke with a smile, "Apologies for disturbing everyone, but today I have some good news to share with you all."

Seeing the crowd all prick up their ears to listen, Li Xiangping continued, "Last night, an immortal from the Azure Pond Immortal Sect passed through. Seeing my younger brother Li Chejing's outstanding innate talent, he accepted him as a disciple and took him to the immortal mountain to study immortal arts."

The moment these words were spoken, it was as if a massive boulder had been thrown into a tranquil lake. The entire assembly was shaken with shock. People exchanged blank stares, gazing at Li Xiangping dumbfoundedly as if listening to a tall tale.

"Congratulations."

It was the teacher, Han Wenxu, who let out a long sigh and loudly offered his congratulations.

Han Wenxu hailed from the city and possessed broad worldly experience. Hearing Li Xiangping utter the name of the Azure Pond Immortal Sect, he knew in his heart that this matter was nine parts true out of ten. Jing'er most likely possessed a spiritual aperture and had been taken away by a cultivator of the Azure Pond Sect.

_Every person has their own karmic destiny._

Han Wenxu sighed softly to himself before speaking up on Li Xiangping's behalf, "For Chejing to enter the immortal sect is truly a blessing for the entire village."

Han Wenxu had lived in the village for over twenty years, practicing medicine to save lives and teaching classes to resolve doubts; he was already an established authority in the village. With him speaking up, Li Xiangping's words finally gained some credibility, though the majority of the villagers still could not help but whisper among themselves.

Li Xiangping's expression remained indifferent. He revealed a jade seal in his hand, and upon catalyzing his dharma power, four glittering golden characters materialized.

«Under Azure Pond's Rule»

"Immortals!"

Witnessing these immortal methods, the crowd immediately erupted into an uproar. Many people repeatedly knelt down to kowtow and cried out loudly. Chen Erniu was also terrified, staring upward with shock and uncertainty.

Watching the character for "Li" finish materializing, Li Xiangping put away the jade seal.

Looking at the crowd below, he announced clearly, "The immortal also bestowed upon us immortal arts, ordering my Li family to guard the villages of Lijing, Lichuankou, Jingyang, and Lidaokou."

Having spoken, he squeezed his palm slightly, and a dazzling golden light emerged from within. With a flick of his wrist, the golden light whistled out, vanishing into the endless mountain forests. With a resounding boom, it startled flocks of birds into the air.

The moment this Golden Light Incantation was unleashed, a swath of people instantly dropped to their knees below. Even the patriarch of the Liu family, Liu Linfeng, knelt down trembling, gazing at him blankly.

"There is no need for this, everyone. We are all relatives and fellow villagers; Xiangping cannot bear such a grand gesture."

Looking down at the kneeling crowd, Li Xiangping remained as steady as a mountain, speaking in a mild tone.

Hearing Li Xiangping's voice, Chen Erniu pressed his forehead tightly against the ground.

Amidst his shock, his mind was a tangled mess, leaving only a few large words echoing in his head, _The heavens are about to change._

---

Li Tongya slowly walked out of Lichuankou. Behind him, Li Yesheng held a roll of cloth in one hand and a charcoal pencil in the other, crookedly jotting something down.

"Lichuankou suffered that drought over twenty years ago, and even now, they have not recovered. There are barely over a hundred households; it is truly pitiful," Li Tongya said to Li Yesheng, turning his head as they walked.

"Brother, we purged the prominent families in Lichuankou and equalized the land. The villagers no longer have rent to pay, so a ten percent land tax is very lenient!" Li Yesheng answered respectfully.

He then handed the charcoal pencil to a villager following behind them, and continued softly, "We will eventually have to send someone to manage affairs here. However, among both the direct and collateral lines of our Li family, there is truly no one left who can read and write well enough to be presented for the role."

"There is no need to overthink it." Li Tongya waved his hand. Under Li Yesheng's envious gaze, he cast a Cleanse Clothing Art, brushing away the dust on his body before answering unhurriedly, "Father and Xiangping will arrange everything properly."
