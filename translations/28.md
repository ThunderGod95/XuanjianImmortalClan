# Chapter 28: The Wan Family

Atop Qingsui Peak sat a small pavilion, its windows seemingly framed by clouds and mists, exquisitely carved like the moon and tailored from the very heavens. The gentle morning light cascaded over the structure, bathing it in a golden glow.

Seated upon a bluestone slab before the pavilion, Li Chejing slowly exhaled a breath of pure qi. He opened his eyes to gaze at the boundless sea of clouds before him.

Raising his handsome brows, he murmured to himself, "This Azure Essence Wheel is finally complete!"

"This dharma technique of our family must have a tremendous origin. What I have cultivated is no ordinary Azure Essence dharma power, but Lunar Azure Essence. Not only is the dharma power dense and solid, but it also vastly surpasses that of ordinary techniques."

Rising to his feet, he smoothed out his azure robes. Li Chejing stepped into the pavilion and took down a gleaming sword from the wall. With a flick of his wrist, he executed a dazzling sword flourish.

_I have finally grasped the basics of the ⟨Profound Water Sword Art⟩ that senior brother gave me. Although a Third Grade technique requires at least the Qi Refinement stage to master, the art itself is fortunately not difficult to comprehend._

_I wonder if Father's health is holding up, and if my brothers have managed to cultivate the Heavenly Circuit Wheel? I heard the Metal Tang Gate to the west has been restless lately. With our borders stretching for thousands of miles, I do not know if our Li family has been caught in the crossfire._

Weighed down by these worries, Li Chejing lost all desire to practice his swordsmanship. He leaned over his desk, absently flipping through a jade slip, his mind already drifting thousands of miles away to the Li family.

"Junior Brother!"

A jovial shout broke his reverie. A man with thick eyebrows and large eyes approached from outside the pavilion. It was his senior brother, Xiao Yuansi.

He looked at Li Chejing at the desk with a smile and spoke, "Junior Brother, guess what good news I bring!"

"Senior Brother, please do not jest."

Li Chejing shook his head with a bitter smile. Stowing the jade slip away, he adopted an attentive posture, ready to listen.

“Master has ordered me to Lixia commandery on an errand,” Xiao Yuansi said, raising an eyebrow before quickly asking, “Do you have a letter for home you’d like me to take?”

"I do, I do! Thank you, Senior Brother! You are far too kind!"

Li Chejing was instantly thrilled. Thanking him profusely, he fetched his writing materials and began writing at once.

Xiao Yuansi chuckled and said softly, "Take your time. Hand it to me tonight."

---

The sky was just beginning to brighten. Li Xiangping slowly roused from his meditation, thinking to himself.

_My sea of qi is now full. With another half-year of cultivation, I can attempt to condense the Heavenly Circuit Wheel._

Calculating the time, he had been stuck at the threshold of the Heavenly Circuit Wheel for nearly four years. His cultivation speed was dreadfully slow. By now, Chejing had likely already begun breaking through to the Azure Essence Wheel.

Stepping out of his room, he found Tian Yun sunbathing by the door. Seeing Li Xiangping approach, she moved to stand.

"Ah, slowly, slowly."

Seeing this, Li Xiangping quickly waved his hands and gently supported Tian Yun's arm, speaking softly, "You are with child now, so you must be extremely careful. Do not disturb the fetal qi!"

Tian Yun smiled gently. Looking into Li Xiangping's eyes, she replied in a soft voice, "Ever since I became pregnant, my body has grown clumsy."

Stifling a yawn, she spoke with a hint of concern, "I hear that Qiuyang is progressing rapidly in his cultivation. I often worry that if our child is born without a spiritual aperture, a century from now, the main branch will weaken while the side branches grow strong. I fear that would spell misfortune for our Li family."

Li Xiangping paused upon hearing this. He affectionately patted her head and burst into laughter.

"Rest assured, our child will possess a spiritual aperture as well. I will take charge of the family's affairs; you have no need to fret."

"As for Li Qiuyang, he merely cultivated the Profound Scenery Wheel a bit earlier. Do not give it too much thought."

Seeing her husband's unwavering confidence, Tian Yun leaned gently into his embrace and whispered, "As long as you know what you are doing."

The couple was enjoying their tender moment when Liu Linfeng suddenly rushed into the front courtyard. Catching sight of them, he hastily skidded to a halt. His face flushed bright red, caught awkwardly between advancing and retreating.

Tian Yun chuckled softly and, supported by Lady Ren, withdrew from the yard.

Li Xiangping stared at Liu Linfeng in slight surprise and asked with a smile, "Uncle, what could be so urgent?"

"One of the Liu family boys under my command ventured beyond Lidaokou and traveled dozens of miles along Guli Road. He stumbled upon a hunting farmer who was incredibly vigilant and bolted the moment he saw our men. The boy didn't dare give chase. He merely noted the location and hurried back to report."

Liu Linfeng panted heavily as he quickly relayed the news to Li Xiangping.

Li Xiangping frowned. Just as he was about to speak, another figure barged hurriedly through the gates. The two focused their eyes and saw that it was Li Yesheng.

Li Yesheng was likewise flushed and gasping for breath, looking as though he had sprinted from one end of the village to the other.

Without daring to rest for a single breath, he blurted out, "Patriarch! Word has arrived from Lidaokou. An immortal who claims to be a cultivator of the Wan Family, under Azure Pond's rule, has come to visit."

"Disaster! They must have tracked the boy here!"

Liu Linfeng slapped his thigh and, filled with regret, dealt himself a harsh slap across the face.

Li Xiangping massaged his temples, let out a long breath, and spoke in a deep voice, "Do not panic just yet. The visitor is also under Azure Pond's rule. They will not resort to immediate violence."

Looking up at the crowd gathering around them, Li Xiangping called out, "Where is Second Brother?"

"The Immortal Master went up the mountain early this morning. He has not yet returned."

"Uncle, make a trip up the mountain. Inform Second Brother of everything that has transpired and tell him to remain at the rear mountain; there is no need for him to come down. Yesheng, you will accompany me to Lidaokou."

Liu Linfeng grunted in affirmation and hastily rushed up the mountain.

With Li Yesheng by his side and a few village guards from Lijing trailing behind, Li Xiangping hurried down the dirt path toward Lidaokou.

Upon arriving at Lidaokou, they ran directly into Xu Wenshan's subordinate. Li Yesheng questioned the man without slowing his pace, "What did the man say?"

"The Immortal Master is entirely silent. He sits in the boss's courtyard without uttering a single word, adjusting his breath with his eyes closed."

The man appeared remarkably calm as he answered.

As they spoke, Li Xiangping and his retinue reached the gates. Smoothing out his robes, he stepped forward and looked toward the figure seated in the place of honor.

It was a boy who looked exceedingly young, perhaps merely in his teens. He had delicate features and wore a long, moon-white robe. At this moment, he opened his eyes to gaze back at Li Xiangping.

"«Under Azure Pond's Rule», Li Xiangping."

Having yet to cultivate the Jade Capital Wheel or awaken his spiritual sense, Li Xiangping could not fathom the boy's strength. He humbly cupped his hands in greeting.

Unexpectedly, the boy seemed to sigh in relief at those words. He quickly stood up and spoke with utmost respect, "«Under Azure Pond's Rule», Wan Yuankai of the Wan Family, greets the Patriarch of the Li family."

"I dare not, I dare not. I am but a minor cultivator of the Li family; the Patriarch is my father."

Li Xiangping waved his hands repeatedly. Seeing the boy behave so humbly, the heavy stone in his heart fell away, and a genuine smile touched his lips.

"A few days ago, my father heard that the exalted one from Qingsui Peak had assigned people to watch over the west of Guli Road. He was overjoyed and sent this humble one westward to investigate, hoping to befriend you all. I happened to run into someone from your esteemed village, and thus came uninvited. I beg your forgiveness."

Wan Yuankai was brimming with excitement. He apologized politely and offered a shallow smile.

"My father also asked me to bring a gift of congratulations."
