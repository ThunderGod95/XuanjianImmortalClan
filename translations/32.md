# Chapter 32: The Visitor

Wan Tiancang's voice was accompanied by falling tears as he described the cruelty of the Ji family and the precarious situation of the Wan family. He stressed that the Wan and Li families would rise together in glory or fall together in ruin.

His words moved even Li Yesheng, who was serving by their side.

However, Li Mutian kept his gaze lowered, entirely unswayed as he gently sipped his tea before speaking, "Has the Wan family's situation truly deteriorated to such a difficult extent?"

"Indeed it has!"

Looking at Li Mutian, Wan Tiancang gritted his teeth. He realized the man before him was a master who would not release the eagle until he saw the hare.

Reaching into his robes, he pulled out an animal skin scroll and said, "Before I left, my older cousin specifically instructed me that, as I will be intruding upon the Li family for my daily needs hereafter, I should bring along an array formation scroll to repay your noble clan's grace."

Li Mutian waved his hand, and Li Tongya stepped forward to receive it. As he did, Wan Tiancang explained, "This array formation is called the Miasma Obscuring Array. My grandfather purchased it at a market one hundred years ago. By utilizing refined array flags, the formation can be deployed. My grandfather was profoundly fond of array formations; the mountain array guarding our ancestral home was laid down by him."

"He originally bought this array to verify his own understanding of the Dao of Arrays. After he passed away in meditation, there has been no one left in the clan proficient in array formations."

Wan Tiancang sighed repeatedly, handing the animal skin scroll over to Li Tongya with both hands, seemingly reluctant to part with it.

Seeing Li Tongya nod, Li Mutian finally smiled and said, "You are too polite. Both the Wan and Li families reside under Azure Pond's rule; it is only natural that we assist each other. Now that the Wan family is facing difficulties, how could the Li family take advantage of your peril? For the harvest, our Li family will only take a one-and-a-half-tenths cut!"

What else could Wan Tiancang say? He merely felt that a grand figure who had undergone Foundation Establishment naturally looked down on this meager offering, only waiving a portion out of courtesy.

With a low, bitter smile, he replied, "Thank you, Senior!"

Li Mutian gave a soft hum of acknowledgment, and Li Tongya led Wan Tiancang, Li Yesheng, and the others out.

Just outside the courtyard gate, Wan Tiancang looked at Li Tongya with a trace of hope and asked, "I wonder where your noble clan has arranged the spirit fields? Please take me to inspect their quality."

"Naturally."

Li Tongya nodded. As they walked along the bluestone path, he explained, "Our Li family has two villages situated along Guli Road. The first is Lidaokou, which lies in the direction Brother Tiancang came from. The second is Lichuankou, located by the shores of Moongaze Lake."

"The spirit fields we are leasing are situated in Lichuankou. I have already ordered men to construct a small courtyard there. The fields are relatively intact, yielding approximately seventy catties annually."

Wan Tiancang nodded to show his understanding. Turning his head, he assessed the scenery of Lijing along the way. "This road is rather well-built. With mortals coming and going, the thoroughfare still remains clean and tidy. That is truly a rare sight."

"Brother Tiancang praises us too highly." Li Tongya gave a low chuckle before continuing, "I have heard there is an immortal market nearby. As our Li family was only recently established, we do not know much about it."

"Brother Tongya, you are always trying to dig more information out of us!" Wan Tiancang laughed heartily, teasing Li Tongya before his expression turned serious. "I have only cultivated the Profound Scenery Wheel and have never visited the market myself. However, my cousin and the Patriarch once went together and shared quite a few details."

"That market is located on a small islet within Moongaze Lake and is run by a rogue cultivator at the peak of Qi Refinement. On the night of the full moon, a large ship travels to the shore to receive rogue cultivators and immortal cultivators from minor families like ours, ferrying them to the islet."

Fearing the Li family might send their people to their deaths and blame the Wan family, Wan Tiancang hastily offered an advice, "According to what the Patriarch said, it is best to reach the Jade Capital Wheel before venturing to that islet, lest any accidents occur along the way."

"We naturally understand," Li Tongya nodded, committing this crucial piece of information to memory as he continued chatting casually with Wan Tiancang.

It was not long before they arrived at Lichuankou. Hearing that Li Tongya had brought a cultivator from another family to the village, Chen Erniu was terrified. He hurriedly brought men to welcome them, trailing carefully behind Li Tongya and conversing in hushed tones with Li Yesheng.

Wan Tiancang estimated the size of the fields, then tested the spiritual energy and soil quality.

He nodded at Li Tongya, signaling his approval. Li Tongya smiled and waved his hand, saying, "Brother Wan must be exhausted from the journey. I still have family matters to attend to, so I will take my leave first."

After giving instructions to Li Yesheng and Chen Erniu to arrange all necessary affairs with Wan Tiancang, he turned and headed back to Lijing.

————

Nowadays, the rear mountain of Lijing had grown far more bustling. The village guards had taken to calling the peak Mount Lijing. Amidst the dense woods, several courtyards had sprouted, their grey bricks and tiled roofs beautifully dotting the landscape.

A winding stone path stretched from the foot of the mountain to its summit. The village guards stationed at the base immediately bowed in deep respect upon seeing Li Tongya, greeting him, "Welcome, Immortal Master."

Giving a casual nod, he treaded the stone path upward. After the time it took an incense stick to burn, a small courtyard leaped into view.

The teacher, Han Wenxu, was currently giving lessons here. The courtyard was filled with half-grown children; a rough glance estimated around fifty or sixty of them, all sitting upright and proper beneath the tree, listening to his lecture.

Not only from Lijing and Jingyang, but children from the further villages of Lidaokou and Lichuankou also came here to study. Only wealthy households with some savings could afford to support one or two children in their studies, and naturally, they could only send them to Lijing.

Gazing at Li Xuanxuan reading inside, Li Tongya pondered inwardly, _In a few more months, the child will be six or seven years old, and his body will have stabilized. We can then test his spiritual aperture. There are still three profound pearl talisman seeds left on the mirror, so I need not fear Xuan'er lacking a spiritual aperture._

_Counting the days, Tian Yun will give birth in a few months as well._

For some unknown reason, the image of Liu Rouxuan suddenly surfaced in Li Tongya's mind. Startled, he shook his head repeatedly to clear the thought. He stood frozen in place for a few seconds before letting out a bitter smile and continuing his trek up the mountain.

Past the schoolhouse lay a flight of one hundred and twenty-six steps. A grand, red-lacquered gate stood upon the platform, and seated by the entrance was an old man with graying hair.

"Uncle Tian!"

Li Tongya greeted him. He found Tian Shoushui sitting cheerfully, a small hammer and a half-finished wooden horse in his hands.

"Tongya, you've returned." Tian Shoushui smiled and nodded. Scratching his head somewhat sheepishly, he pointed at the wooden horse in his hands. "Yun'er is about to give birth, so I thought I would make a little something for the child to play with."

The old man had suffered numerous injuries in his youth, and as he aged, the years caught up with him quickly. Though he was roughly the same age as Li Mutian, he looked more than a decade older.

"Alright, take your time making it."

Li Tongya held deep respect for this elder who was like a father to him. After exchanging greetings, he stepped into the courtyard.

Li Xiangping was slowly awakening from meditation. Exhaling a breath of impure qi, he looked at Li Tongya and said, "Second Brother has achieved the Heavenly Circuit Wheel?"

"Yes," Li Tongya arched a brow, replying with a smile.

"You are still one step ahead of me." Li Xiangping feigned depression, but couldn't help smiling himself as he stood up and stretched.

After recounting the matter concerning Wan Tiancang and placing the array formation scroll on the table, Li Tongya picked up a teabowl from the table and took a sip.

Seeing Li Xiangping lower his head in deep thought, Li Tongya asked, "What is it?"

"Wan Tiancang is much harder to deal with than Wan Yuankai," Li Xiangping raised his head and said with a calm expression.

Li Tongya gave a light chuckle. Just as he was about to speak, Tian Shoushui poked his head in and called out, "Linfeng is here!"

The two exchanged a puzzled glance. Soon, they saw Liu Linfeng hurrying into the courtyard. Wiping away his sweat, he looked at the brothers and said, "An Immortal Master has arrived at the foot of the mountain. He claims to be an envoy from Qingsui Peak."
