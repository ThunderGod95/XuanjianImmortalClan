# Chapter 34: The Fog Bewildering Array

`Xiao Yuansi held up the array diagram, studied it for a dozen or so breaths, and shook his head, saying,` "And here I thought it was some novel plaything. It is nothing more than the Fog Bewildering `Array framework` under a different name."

Stowing the array diagram and handing it back to Li Xiangping, Xiao Yuansi continued, "Though I do not know much about the Dao of Arrays, this array diagram is far too simplistic. `It might deceive cultivators at the Fetal Breathing realm, and those who have cultivated the Jade Capital Wheel could easily retreat unscathed. But if you encounter a cultivator at Qi Refinement, they would find it effortless to break the array formation and seize the flags.`"

"Our Li family is utterly destitute. Having this array formation will at least grant us some peace of mind," Li Xiangping cupped his hands and smiled bitterly.

Looking at Li Xiangping, who was merely at the Bearing Brightness Wheel, and Li Tongya, who was at the Heavenly Circuit Wheel, Xiao Yuansi could not help but laugh. "For the two of you, it is indeed not a bad choice."

With a light tap on his brocade pouch, six small flags suddenly appeared in Xiao Yuansi's hand. These small flags had profound incantations painted on their blue and white fabric. Their shafts were brownish-red, giving them quite a fine appearance.

"These are the array flags I used when I was at the Jade Capital stage. The flags are made from the skin of a white-striped carp, and the shafts are of red birch wood. They are considered `quite decent treasures for the Fetal Breathing realm and are quite compatible` with your array formation."

"Since I have no use for this flag array anymore, I will leave it here to set up a Fog Bewildering Array for you. In the future, if you find a better array diagram, you can also rely on these array flags to deploy it."

Xiao Yuansi smiled, waving his hand to stop Li Xiangping and Li Tongya as they were about to thank him.

He spoke up, "Twelve spirit stones."

Li Xiangping was immediately struck dumb. He chuckled awkwardly and asked, "I wonder what these spirit stones..."

"You do not have spirit stones?" Xiao Yuansi paused, then explained, "Among immortal cultivators, spirit stones are just like the gold and silver of mortals. Transactions in markets or shops are typically settled with spirit stones, and larger families also require them when paying tributes."

Hearing this, Li Xiangping's heart stirred, and he quickly inquired, "May I ask how many spirit stones this White Essence Fruit and the spirit rice can be exchanged for?"

"If you use those two items as collateral..." Xiao Yuansi rubbed his chin, pondered for a moment, and replied, "Ten White Essence Fruits should be worth one spirit stone. As for spirit rice, one hundred catties would equal one spirit stone."

Li Xiangping and Li Tongya were immediately rendered speechless. They thought inwardly, _Even a hundred catties of spirit rice was enough to force the Wan family to dispatch a cultivator to rent our fields. These twelve spirit stones equate to a full twelve hundred catties of spirit rice! I fear even if we sold our entire Li family, it would not be worth this sum._

Seeing the two men lower their heads in silence, Xiao Yuansi instantly understood. He said softly, "Very well. Consider this Miasma Obscuring Array traded to me; I will count it as two spirit stones. As for the rest, I will put it on your tab. Once your family gathers enough, entrust it to the Senior Brothers who receive the tributes in Lixia commandery to send to Qingsui Peak."

Li Xiangping gave a bitter smile and bowed in gratitude. He then watched as Xiao Yuansi wove a dharma seal, causing the six array flags in his hand to float into the air.

"Go!"

With a gentle push from Xiao Yuansi, five of the six array flags took to the sky, trailing faint white flames. As if possessing a mind of their own, they scattered and plunged into the bamboo forest at the foot of the mountain.

In an instant, thick, grey mist billowed and spread at the base of the mountain, completely shrouding Mount Lijing layer by layer.

Before long, the grey mist gradually thinned out. Viewed from afar, the people on the mountain had vanished. The entire mountain seemed frozen in this moment, devoid of any further changes.

"It is done! What I have deployed for you is the sect's Fog Bewildering Array, which is somewhat superior to that Miasma Obscuring Array."

Xiao Yuansi withdrew his hand and plunged the final array flag into the ground. Immediately, a palm-sized array core materialized, divided into an outer and inner layer, shimmering with a faint white light.

Xiao Yuansi clapped his hands and pointed at the array core, explaining to the men, "This array core is divided into the outer array and the inner eye. If the outer array flashes brightly, it means someone is trying to intrude from the foot of the mountain. If the inner eye flashes brightly, that person has already broken through the array formation and entered."

"Once you have cultivated the Jade Capital Wheel, you can use your spiritual sense to refine the array core and control the array formation freely. Right now, with only the cultivation of the Heavenly Circuit Wheel, you can merely drip a drop of blood from your fingertip onto the array flag of this core so the array formation will recognize you."

"Thank you, Immortal Master!" Li Tongya and Li Xiangping hurried forward to drip their fresh blood. The array core flickered, and the two men instantly felt a faint connection with it.

Witnessing this miraculous scene, Li Xiangping's initially heavy heart from taking on debt lifted considerably. He pondered inwardly, _This Fog Bewildering Array is far better than expected. It can conceal our mountain gate and block the prying eyes of mortals. These array flags can also be reused. It seems these ten spirit stones were money well spent._

_Once the younger generation grows up, the spirit fields in the village can all be utilized. It simply means taking a few more years to pay it off, but securing this mountain gate is of paramount importance! Especially regarding that mirror..._

Xiao Yuansi dispelled his technique and asked with a smile, "Do you have any more doubts?"

Seeing them shake their heads repeatedly, Xiao Yuansi let out a long laugh and said softly, "Then I shall take a stroll through the mountains, slay a few demons, and gather some spirit herbs. I will return at dawn tomorrow to open the furnace."

"Farewell, Immortal Master!" Li Xiangping and the others hastily called out, watching as Xiao Yuansi summoned a flying shuttle and soared into the sky upon it.

Waiting until Xiao Yuansi was far away, Li Tongya turned to Liu Linfeng and said, "Uncle. This academy is no longer suitable to be on the mountain. Move it down to the foot of the mountain tonight."

"I will go pack things up," Liu Linfeng acknowledged and headed down.

Li Tongya looked at Li Xiangping and said, "Tomorrow, we should take that mirr—"

"Brother!" Li Xiangping, suspicious by nature, was still wary that Xiao Yuansi had not gone far and quickly interrupted him.

Li Tongya instantly understood. The two exchanged a glance, sat cross-legged, and began to cultivate.

Although Mount Lijing was not a famous mountain, it was still a branch of the Great Li Mountains and had nurtured a spirit root like the Serpent Dragon Fruit. With the array formation isolating the flow of spiritual energy between the inside and outside, the spiritual mechanisms and earth vein were invigorated. The concentration of spiritual energy gradually rose until it was ten percent higher than the outside world before slowly coming to a halt.

The two men, deep in cultivation, were instantly pleasantly surprised, their satisfaction with the array formation growing even deeper.

They engaged in breathing and cultivation until brilliant golden light rose from the east. The mountain mist slowly dissipated, condensing on the treetops to form drops of dew.

Only then did Xiao Yuansi descend from the sky, his robes immaculate as he slowly landed in the courtyard atop his flying shuttle.

"Time to open the furnace and refine the pills!" he laughed heartily, summoning a pitch-black, human-sized alchemy furnace from his storage bag.

Sitting cross-legged, he said to the brothers, "Although these Serpent Essence Pills bear the name of the dragon, their nature is gentle. Even though they are pills for the Qi Refinement stage, those in the Fetal Breathing realm can also consume them. They will be of great benefit to the two of you."

Taking out a piece of brownish-yellow wood from his storage bag, Xiao Yuansi brought his hands together and summoned a streak of pure white alchemy fire from his dantian. He lightly brushed the alchemy fire in his hands across the block of wood. The wood instantly ignited with a crackle, revealing a faint blue flame.

Tossing the wooden block to the bottom of the furnace, Xiao Yuansi took out the jade box, used his dharma power to extract the Serpent Dragon Fruit, and cast it into the furnace.

Once the Serpent Dragon Fruit had completely melted within the furnace, dissolving into a pool of red medicinal liquid floating above the alchemy fire, Xiao Yuansi took out several supplementary herbs and tossed them into the furnace one by one.

Finally, he continuously refined the mixture using the alchemy fire. After about two hours, when the thin mist in the mountains had dispersed and the dew had entirely evaporated, eight pale red spirit pills were spat out from the furnace's spout.

Xiao Yuansi wove a dharma seal, and the eight pills immediately flew up, landing in his white-glowing palm. He took out eight small white jade bottles and individually packaged the pills.

Keeping two bottles for himself, he handed the rest to Li Xiangping and Li Tongya, explaining, "These Serpent Essence Pills must also be stored in jade bottles and kept carefully. Only open the wooden stopper when you are ready to consume them. If they are not sealed tightly, the medicinal efficacy will gradually dissipate, wasting a pill for nothing."

"If kept sealed, these pills can be preserved for about twenty years, so there is no need to worry."

Li Xiangping and Li Tongya naturally agreed and carefully accepted them. Li Tongya then exchanged a glance with Li Xiangping, took out three bottles of the Serpent Essence Pills, and said, "Immortal Master, please take these three bottles of spirit pills to our younger brother so he can cultivate properly."

"Not bad. Your Li family values familial bonds. I will certainly deliver them," Xiao Yuansi nodded approvingly. 

Accepting the three jade bottles, he cupped his hands and spoke with a touch of emotion, "The Li family stepped onto the immortal path only a few years ago, so your foundation is still somewhat shallow. The clan's Dao of Alchemy, Dao of Arrays, and artifact forging arts still require people to inherit them. You must also absorb more individuals possessing spiritual apertures into the clan so that you will not fade away after a few generations... There are no dominant cultivator families along Lixia Road and Guli Road, so the Li family must seize this opportunity."

"Over the past few centuries, countless families in Lixia commandery have risen abruptly, only to vanish hastily a few generations later. As a prominent family in the commandery, my Xiao family has sat by and watched countless banquets begin and end, countless pavilions rise and fall. The Li family must encourage yourselves all the more..."

Xiao Yuansi's heartfelt words moved Li Tongya. He let out a long sigh and said, "The Immortal Master speaks the truth. We brothers built this from scratch. Today, there are only twenty or thirty disciples surnamed Li, and the branch Ye family barely has two or three hundred people. Jing'er cultivates alone in the sect, yet we are unable to provide him with any cultivation resources."

Seeing Li Tongya sighing endlessly, Xiao Yuansi furrowed his brows and said softly, "My mastery in the Dao of Alchemy was inherited from within the sect, and I have sworn the Profound Scenery Spirit Oath. The recipe for the Serpent Essence Pills cannot be passed to outsiders. You might try searching the markets; perhaps you will have some gains."

Hearing this, Li Xiangping, who had been waiting on the side for quite a while, quickly inquired, "Dare I ask the Immortal Master, this Profound Scenery Spirit Oath..."

Xiao Yuansi was taken aback for a moment before explaining, "Clans and sects all use this Profound Scenery Spirit Oath to secretly pass down cultivation methods. The caster swears by their Profound Scenery Wheel, the oath originating from the heart. If the oath is broken, their Profound Scenery shatters, and their entire cultivation pours out like rushing water. Within moments, they will regress to a mortal, their qi withered and spirit exhausted, dying as a consequence of the oath."

"This Profound Scenery Spirit Oath is of great use for preserving inheritances. I wonder what the price..." Li Tongya quickly followed up, but Xiao Yuansi waved his hand to interrupt him.

"It is just a worthless little trick that everyone knows. I will write it down for you."

He accepted the brush and paper, wielded the brush smoothly across the paper, and finished writing in the time it took to drink a cup of tea.

He softly reminded them, "However, you must note one thing. This spirit oath is not the luminous Heavenly Dao that perceives all things. If used to keep dharma techniques or secrets confidential, it works fine. But if you intend to use it to prevent the other party from harboring malicious intentions, it is of little use."

"After all, even the Heavenly Dao struggles to distinguish between human good and evil; what could a mere spirit oath understand?"

"Thank you for your guidance, Immortal Master! Our Li family will remember this kindness!" The two men thanked him profusely.

The heavy stone of constant vigilance in Li Xiangping's heart finally fell, and his mood lightened considerably. 

He pondered inwardly, _With this Profound Scenery Spirit Oath, the final link is complete. Once those two children cultivate the Profound Scenery Wheel and swear the oath, we can use them without worry._

Xiao Yuansi took the reply letter handed over by Li Xiangping, stowed his alchemy furnace, and summoned his flying shuttle.

He chuckled lightly, "My junior brother's cultivation talent is quite good. Though his root bone is only average, his talent in sword arts is absolutely outstanding. You two need not worry."

"Fellow Daoists, until fate brings us together again!"

With that, he mounted his flying shuttle and soared into the sky amidst their chorused gratitude.

Once Xiao Yuansi was far away, Li Xiangping carefully tucked the remaining three bottles of pills into his robes and smiled at Li Tongya. "Jing'er truly has a good Senior Brother."

Li Tongya nodded. While reading and memorizing the Profound Scenery Spirit Oath, he handed the jade slip to Li Xiangping, gesturing for him to take a look.

Li Xiangping hurriedly accepted the jade slip, gently unfurling it. Staring at the dense, fly-head-sized characters crowded on the slip, he immediately froze.

After forcing himself to read several dozen words, he muttered in a dumbfounded voice, "And he calls this 'not difficult'?"

Li Xuanxuan was absent-minded during class, staring blankly at Han Wenxu, who was speaking eloquently on the podium.

Han Wenxu was nearing fifty, and white hair was gradually creeping up his temples. In his twenties, while working as an apprentice in an apothecary within the commandery, he had killed a man in a fit of rage. Forced to flee, he hid in the Great Li Mountains. For over twenty years, he had treated the villagers' illnesses and taught the children, living a rather fulfilling life.

Looking at the children reading earnestly below, a smile touched Han Wenxu's face. He was childless his whole life, but having raised the children of the Li family, these kids growing up in his schoolhouse were no different from his own sons and nephews.

"Hm? Is it getting foggy?"

Han Wenxu looked at the thin layer of mist at his feet and thought with some unease, _Why would fog roll in at this hour..._

Below the podium, the somewhat drowsy Li Xuanxuan had sharp eyes. He suddenly jolted awake, tugging at the corner of the equally sleepy Li Xiewen's clothes, and whispered, "Brother Wen! Look out the window."

Li Xiewen quickly looked over and saw layers of grey mist billowing outside the window. Feeling somewhat uneasy, he whispered back, "Brother, what is going on?"

Furrowing his small brows, Li Xuanxuan spoke with gravity, "This mist is far too suspicious, and the adults of our family are at the summit. Let us inform the teacher and go up the mountain together to notify them."

He was not yet six years old, but his manner of speaking already carried the bearing of an eight or nine-year-old.

Just as Li Xuanxuan finished speaking, they saw Great-Uncle Liu Linfeng enter the schoolhouse. He whispered something to Han Wenxu, who quickly nodded and announced, "Children, pack your clothes and bedding. Starting today, you have a three-day holiday."

The two boys exchanged a glance and quickly went to pack their belongings amidst the cheers of the other children.

After notifying Han Wenxu, Liu Linfeng sat on the steps in front of the building, waiting for the children. Suddenly feeling an itch in his mouth, he reached into his robes for his tobacco pipe.

As he raised his head, he faintly spotted a figure craning its neck in the mist, crouching in the woods and peering around stealthily.

Liu Linfeng was instantly frightened out of his wits and jumped up from the ground. His precious tobacco pipe dropped and snapped its mouthpiece on the floor.

Without time to feel heartache over it, he bellowed with a trembling voice, "Who is there!!"
