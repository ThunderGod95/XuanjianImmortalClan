# Chapter 39: Supreme Yin Profound Light

"This ⟨Moon Lake Autumn Reflection Art⟩ was once a dharma technique passed down from the Immortal Mansion. It is incredibly miraculous and requires two types of worldly spiritual qi. The first is called Supreme Yin Lunar Essence, and the second is called Golden Autumn on Lake. The sect has some reserves of the Golden Autumn on Lake, but as for the Supreme Yin Lunar Essence, it has not been seen for hundreds of years ever since the Immortal Mansion sealed itself away from the world."

"That Moon Lake Peak Master from centuries ago only managed to cultivate this technique after serendipitously receiving a reward from the Immortal Mansion. Since his death, no one has been able to practice it successfully."

Li Chejing had also heard rumors of that Immortal Mansion. It was said to be a colossal power spanning across multiple states, though it had not appeared in the world for centuries.

Sighing softly in his heart, he nodded and expressed his thanks, "So that is the case. Thank you, Senior Brother, for clarifying my doubts!"

---

Lu Jiangxian's divine sense permeated through the mirror's surface, observing the child who was currently circulating the ⟨Reception Method⟩.

A faint, ethereal white gleam materialized above the boy's head. It was about seven inches long and emitted a subtle radiance.

_This child's compatibility with the Profound Pearl Talisman Seed is quite good, but I wonder how his cultivation talent is._

Lu Jiangxian withdrew his divine sense from Li Xuanxuan. Suddenly, he felt a powerful tremor within his core. His vision blurred, and it was as if a pure, jade-white disc had materialized before his eyes.

An immediate realization struck him, _That child Li Chejing has broken through to the Jade Capital Wheel!_

An unfathomably mysterious power surged across thousands of miles through the connection between the Profound Pearl Talisman Seeds, sinking gently into the grayish-blue mirror on the stone platform. Lu Jiangxian felt his spirit lighten as a stream of lunar-white light surfaced.

When Li Tongya and Li Xiangping had broken through previously, tiny amounts of power had fed back into Lu Jiangxian's mirror. Now, coupled with Li Chejing's numerous breakthroughs, this final feedback from attaining the Jade Capital Wheel allowed Lu Jiangxian to finally condense the Supreme Yin Profound Light.

_This profound light roughly contains the power of a concentrated strike from a full Jade Capital Wheel cultivation base, but its attack range is immense. It even exceeds my current divine sense coverage._

Having cultivated this profound light, Lu Jiangxian's divine sense range expanded to encompass the entirety of Lijing village. He was thoroughly thrilled; the body of the mirror grew slightly hot, and a faint, subtle pull surfaced in his mind once again.

_With the expansion of my divine sense, I can perceive things much further away!_

Lu Jiangxian carefully sensed the direction and pondered, _It seems to still be on Moongaze Lake, just much further out than before. Should I hint to the Li family to go take a look?_

_No._ Lu Jiangxian's heart chilled, and he reasoned inwardly, _Judging by the location, it seems to be within the market upon the lake. It is best to wait until the two Li brothers have cultivated the Jade Capital Wheel before going to investigate._

---

With a creak, the door opened as Li Mutian and the two Li brothers entered the room. Li Xiangping patted Li Xuanxuan's shoulder, his expression turning serious.

"Are you prepared?"

"I am ready!"

Li Xuanxuan nodded forcefully, handing the wooden slip back to Li Xiangping.

Li Mutian watched as Li Xiangping stowed away the wooden slip. Only then did he respectfully invite the mirror onto the altar desk, lighting several sticks of incense before it.

"Li family disciple Li Xuanxuan respectfully requests the Profound Brilliance Wondrous Dharma to govern my destiny and soothe my spirit, as I dedicate myself to pursuing the Dao."

"I shall perform meritorious deeds at the proper times, failing not in my efficacy and faith. As the talisman burns, my body offers its gratitude to the Supreme Yin."

As soon as Li Xuanxuan's voice fell, Lu Jiangxian activated the dharma technique. Brilliant, multicolored light gleamed across the mirror's surface, pulsing rhythmically like breathing, before abruptly spitting out a white pellet.

Li Xuanxuan hastily sat down cross-legged, guiding his dharma technique to receive the talisman seed.

Li Tongya looked at Li Xuanxuan on the ground, then turned to secure the door tighter as he spoke softly, "Liu Rouxuan attained the Profound Scenery Wheel last night. In a few more months, Xuanxuan will also reach the Profound Scenery Wheel. By that calculation, our Li family will soon have six cultivators."

"Two Heavenly Circuit Wheels, three Profound Scenery Wheels, and Chejing should have already attained the Jade Capital Wheel in the immortal sect."

Li Xiangping gazed at the grayish-blue mirror overflowing with multicolored light on the desk, adding, "The main branch of our Li family still lacks higher-level cultivators. Let alone Ji Dengqi in Qi Refinement, even the neighboring Wan family has a peak Jade Capital Wheel cultivator."

"Only when a Jade Capital Wheel cultivator awakens their spiritual sense can they refine pills, forge artifacts, lay formations, and use storage bags. Entering and leaving the market is only safe then."

As Lu Jiangxian listened to their conversation, his divine sense suddenly stirred. He faintly detected an unfamiliar aura approaching from the west, pacing uncertainly not far away.

Lu Jiangxian's perception was divided into two layers. The first was called visual sense, which had an immense range, vaguely covering the entire Li family's territory from Lichuankou to Lidaokou. This visual sense was equivalent to a mortal's "sight," only able to observe and probe rough images. However, the advantage was that this kind of probing would generally go undetected; even the Foundation Establishment cultivator Si Yuanbai had not noticed his peeking back then.

The second layer was his divine sense, which covered an area about the size of Lijing village. It was akin to a mortal's "touch." Within this range, Lu Jiangxian could use his divine sense to scan a person entirely, but depending on their respective strengths, the other party might experience a distinct feeling of being watched. Likewise, within his divine sense range, he could unleash the Supreme Yin Profound Light to strike down enemies.

Now, his visual sense detected that this aura, approximately possessing the strength of a Jade Capital Wheel cultivator, was sneaking around near Lichuankou.

Feeling a surge of unease, he muttered inwardly, _Why is a cultivator with a Jade Capital Wheel cultivation base hovering around here... I should alert the Li family._

With a shift in his thoughts, silver-white lunar essence gushed from the mirror's surface, weaving together into strands of moon-white threads that gathered in the air.

This sudden change gave Li Tongya and the others quite a fright. Watching the silver light continuously coalescing in midair, they could not help but glance at one another in bewilderment.

Li Mutian narrowed his eyes and said in a deep voice, "Do not panic. Let us see clearly first."

"It looks like a map of our Li family's territory," Li Tongya said hesitantly, stroking his chin.

Indeed, the lunar essence gradually condensed into a bright white map. The villages of Lichuankou, Lidaokou, and Jingyang were all present above, appearing exceedingly exquisite.

However, only the location of Lichuankou continuously flashed with a brilliant silver light.

"Lichuankou?" Li Xiangping murmured to himself.

---

Chen Erniu had been managing affairs in Lichuankou for several years. He had grown a size wider and sprouted a beard on his chin, finally looking less like a simple farmer.

During the day, he had finished tallying the field accounts and settled a land dispute between two brothers. Now, lying comfortably on his bed, he happily began contemplating tomorrow's itinerary.

_Those few plots of land to the east of the village are decent enough. Tomorrow, I'll send some men to clear them and claim a few more plots._

Lichuankou and Lidaokou bordered each other closely. Administratively, they did not have a superior-subordinate dynamic like the adjacent Lijing and Jingyang villages. Therefore, it naturally fell upon him, as the manager, to fiercely negotiate and fight for their interests in all matters.

That Xu Wenshan had originally intended to pioneer a trade route. Who would have thought that the Wan family was on the verge of losing even the Guli Road to the Ji family? Naturally, there was no path eastward. This had infuriated the man, making him exceedingly volatile these past few months, constantly seeking to lock horns with him, Chen Erniu, over every little thing.

_What a nuisance._

Just as he was pondering this, Chen Erniu heard an uproar outside.

His eldest son, fraught with panic, rushed in hastily and shouted, "Disaster, disaster! Father! Someone died at the edge of the village!"

Chen Erniu quickly jumped up from the bed, crying out in alarm, "What?"
